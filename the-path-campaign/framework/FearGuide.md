# GM Fear Guide

## UNIVERSAL FEAR SPENDING OPTIONS

### Stress & Pressure
- **Spend 2 Fear** → Give the whole party 1 Stress
- **Spend 1 Fear** → Give the whole party 1 Stress (if situation is particularly dire or thematic)
- **Spend 1 Fear** → Give a single PC 1 Stress during a tense moment

### Mist Mechanics
- **Spend 2 Fear (Session Start)** → Make one location on the Path Mist-Touched
- **Spend 3 Fear (Mid-Session)** → Make a currently Unstable location Mist-Touched
- **Gain 2 Fear (Mid-Session)** <- When the players collect flame into their flame Canisters
