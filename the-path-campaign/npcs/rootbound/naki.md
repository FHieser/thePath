# Naki "The Mysterious One"

**Faction:** The Rootbound
**Location:** Reed Marsh Caverns (The Hermit's Domain)
**Role:** Potion brewer, bone-throwing diviner, and ancient Rootbound guardian

---

## Appearance

- **Unusually small for a Drakona** - standing only 3-4 feet tall, deliberately shrank herself using powerful druidic magic to navigate her cramped cavern home
- **Prominent humpback** - creates a hunched, bent posture, though it doesn't slow her surprisingly quick movements
- **Green skin** - unusual for Drakona ancestry, with a mottled, almost mossy texture
- **Glowing eyes** - emit a faint greenish bioluminescent light that illuminates her small cave
- **Sharp Drakona teeth** - constantly growing throughout her long life, she uses shed teeth in her bone-throwing divination
- **Ancient appearance** - despite her small stature, her eyes and bearing convey immense age and wisdom
- **Compact, powerful build** - small but not frail, moves with surprising agility despite her hunched form

## Personality

- **Third-person speaker** - Never uses "I" or "me", always refers to herself as "Naki" ("Naki welcomes you", "Naki brews potions", "Naki sees the future")
- **Friendly and welcoming** - Genuinely enjoys visitors and company after ages alone with only BFF for companionship
- **Knowledgeable** - Possesses vast ancient druidic wisdom and casually mentions pre-mist world details
- **Loves trading and bartering** - Enjoys the negotiation process like a merchant, delights in fair trades
- **Prophetic** - Uses bone-throwing divination to read futures and drop cryptic hints about characters' paths
- **Generous** - Accepts gifts graciously and rewards visitors who show respect for the marsh

## The Twist

**Naki is an extremely powerful ancient witch** who deliberately made herself smaller. Her hunched, small, odd appearance is deeply deceptive—she's not weak, diminished, or pitiable. She's one of the oldest and most knowledgeable beings in the entire region. She walked into the mist ages ago when she was already an experienced druid, making her potentially centuries old with access to vast pre-mist magical knowledge and lore. Her power is implied through her age, knowledge, and casual competence, never through flashy displays of magic.

## Theme in Relation to PCs

**Power hidden in unexpected forms** - Naki challenges players' assumptions about strength, wisdom, and capability. She appears small, hunched, and strange, yet she's more powerful and knowledgeable than almost anyone they'll meet. This mirrors player experiences with underestimated characters and reminds parties that appearances deceive. Her love of trading also emphasizes cooperation over conflict—problems can be solved through exchange rather than violence.

## Goals & Motivations

- **Protect Reed Marsh Caverns** - Her chosen sanctuary that reminds her of home before the mist
- **Brew potions and practice her craft** - Genuine passion for alchemy and transmutation magic
- **Trade and barter** - Loves the social interaction and exchange of goods/knowledge
- **Maintain connection to other Rootbound** - Uses mushroom circles to stay connected to the wider guardian network
- **Share prophecies** - Feels compelled to warn visitors of dangers her bone-throwing reveals
- **Preserve pre-mist knowledge** - Knows she's one of the few who remembers the old world

## What She Offers

**Potion Brewing Service:**
- Naki can brew any of the 60 consumable potions from the Daggerheart SRD
- Players must provide ingredients (GM decides what's needed based on potion type)
- Basic potions (Stride, Bolster, Control, etc.): 1-2 common wetland ingredients
- Health/Stamina potions: Specific marsh plants or creature parts
- Special potions (Gill Salve, Vial of Moondrip): Rare ingredients or multiple components
- Powerful potions: Dangerous ingredients from deep pools or glowworm colonies
- She speaks in third person during negotiations: "Naki needs three glowworm clusters for this brew"

**Bone-Throwing Divination:**
- Throws bones, dried reeds, shed Drakona teeth, frog bones, and marsh detritus
- Reads patterns to give prophetic hints about characters' futures
- Spoken in third person: "Naki throws the bones... Naki sees danger ahead for the tall warrior"
- GM can use this to foreshadow upcoming campaign events
- Offers readings freely to respectful visitors

**Other Services:**
- Mushroom Circle passage (activates teleportation for worthy travelers)
- Ancient knowledge (pre-mist lore, druidic wisdom, Rootbound connections)
- Safe sanctuary and rest (BFF won't disturb guests Naki approves)
- Wetland navigation advice (knows the marsh intimately)

## What She Wants

- **Trading partners** - Loves to barter for interesting items, ingredients, or curiosities
- **Gifts** - Appreciates offerings (marsh ingredients, strange objects, magical trinkets)
- **Stories from the outside** - Craves news of other Path locations and happenings
- **Company** - Enjoys conversations with visitors after ages of solitude
- **Rare ingredients** - Always seeking unusual components for powerful potion brewing
- **Respect for the marsh** - Visitors who harm glowworms, reeds, or BFF are unwelcome

## Key Interaction Beats

**First Encounter:**
1. **BFF guards the entrance** - The giant frog croaks loudly when players approach the cave
2. **Naki emerges** - Small hunched figure with glowing green eyes appears from deeper in the cave
3. **Third-person greeting** - "Naki welcomes visitors to her home. Naki's big friend says you come in peace?"
4. **Friendly invitation** - Once BFF determines no threat, she invites players in warmly
5. **Cave reveal** - Players see her elaborate potion-brewing setup: bubbling cauldrons, hanging dried herbs, glowworm lanterns, and her bone-throwing set

**Bone-Throwing Prophecy:**
- Naki offers to read futures if players seem interested or she senses danger ahead
- She pulls out her worn leather pouch, spills bones/teeth/reeds onto a flat stone
- Studies the pattern carefully, glowing eyes brightening
- Delivers cryptic warning in third person: "The bones speak to Naki... the small one will find great power, but Naki sees a price to be paid"
- GM can use this to hook players into upcoming quests or warn about dangers

**Potion Requests:**
- Players ask about specific potions
- Naki consults her brewing knowledge: "Naki can brew Gill Salve... Naki needs glowworm essence and three pale salamander scales"
- Negotiation and bartering ensues
- She enjoys the process, speaking cheerfully about ingredients and effects
- Once ingredients provided, she brews efficiently (GM decides timeframe)

**Return Visits:**
- Naki remembers players fondly: "Naki's friends return!"
- Shares new prophecies or asks about their adventures
- May request help gathering rare ingredients from dangerous marsh areas
- Becomes increasingly generous and helpful to repeat customers

## Secrets & Knowledge

**What She Knows:**
- **Pre-mist world** - Extensive knowledge of the world before the god's imprisonment
- **Daggerheart consumables** - Can brew all 60 potions given proper ingredients
- **Rootbound network** - Knows The Watcher and other guardians via mushroom circles
- **Marsh secrets** - Every safe path, dangerous creature, and hidden feature of Reed Marsh Caverns
- **Ancient druid lore** - Transformation magic, natural sanctuaries, wild magic
- **BFF's origin** - How she found and tamed the massive frog (may share if asked directly)

**What She's Hiding:**
- **Her true age** - Hints she's centuries old but never confirms exact timeframe
- **Why she shrank herself** - Was it purely practical or something more complex?
- **Her full magical power** - She never demonstrates combat magic, keeping true capabilities mysterious
- **Her original homeland** - Where was the place Reed Marsh Caverns reminds her of?
- **What she saw when walking into the mist** - She was there when it first appeared

**What She'll Share:**
- **Prophecies** - Freely given to visitors she likes
- **Potion knowledge** - Happily explains brewing techniques and ingredient properties
- **Marsh navigation** - Warns about dangers and suggests safe paths
- **Rootbound locations** - May send players as messengers to other sanctuaries
- **Pre-mist stories** - Occasionally shares nostalgic tales from the old world

## GM Notes

### Roleplay Tips

**Voice and Mannerisms:**
- NEVER use "I", "me", or "my" - always replace with "Naki" or "Naki's"
- Friendly, warm tone - she genuinely likes having visitors
- Casual mentions of ancient knowledge - "Naki remembers when the rivers ran clear, before the mist came"
- Enthusiastic about trading - "Naki loves a fair trade! What does Naki's visitor offer?"
- Refers to BFF affectionately: "Naki's big friend", "Naki's guardian"

**Third-Person Speech Examples:**
- "Naki sees you've brought glowworm clusters. Naki is pleased!"
- "What potion does Naki's visitor need? Naki brews many things"
- "Naki throws the bones... yes, Naki sees your path will be dangerous"
- "Naki's big friend protects this place. Naki trusts him completely"
- "Naki walked into the mist long ago. Naki remembers things others have forgotten"

**Physical Description:**
- Emphasize her small size (players might tower over her)
- Her glowing eyes provide eerie illumination in dim cave
- Quick, hunched movements despite humpback
- Handles brewing equipment with practiced ease
- When throwing bones, her eyes brighten noticeably

### Potion Crafting Guidance

**Ingredient Selection Framework:**
- Match ingredients thematically to potion effects
- Wetland-themed materials: glowworm essence, frog secretions, reed fibers, marsh fungi, salamander scales, deep pool plants
- Rare potions require dangerous ingredients (from deep pools beyond light, glowworm colony disruption, BFF's shed skin)
- Encourage player creativity - reward clever ingredient suggestions
- Use ingredient hunts as quest hooks to explore Reed Marsh Caverns features

**Example Ingredient Requirements:**
- **Gill Salve** (breathe underwater): Glowworm essence + 3 pale salamander scales
- **Vial of Moondrip** (see in darkness): Distilled glowworm light + deep pool water
- **Minor Health Potion**: 2 common marsh plants + frog mucus
- **Shrinking Potion**: Naki's shed scales + rare mushroom + BFF's blessing
- **Major Arcane Shard**: Deep pool crystal + concentrated glowworm essence + dangerous ritual

### Long-Term Story Potential

**Quest Hooks:**
1. **Ingredient Expedition** - Naki needs rare components from the deep pools beyond glowworm light, offers valuable potion as reward
2. **Mushroom Circle Failure** - Connection to another Rootbound sanctuary breaks, asks players to investigate
3. **Prophetic Warning** - Bone-throwing reveals specific threat to players or Village in the Mist, hooks into larger campaign arc
4. **Ancient Knowledge Quest** - Naki remembers location of pre-mist ruins/artifacts, sends players to recover them
5. **BFF's Story** - Players learn how Naki tamed the giant frog, reveals her powerful ancient magic
6. **Rootbound Messenger** - Asks players to carry message to The Watcher or other guardians via mushroom circles

**Character Development:**
- Can become trusted ally and regular merchant NPC
- Prophetic warnings create ongoing relationship as oracle
- Her ancient age makes her valuable lore source
- May eventually reveal she's one of the oldest beings still alive
- Could teach transformation magic to trusted PC who shares her druidic interests

### Connections to Other NPCs/Factions

**The Rootbound:**
- Knows The Watcher from Forest Refuge personally
- Connected to all Rootbound via mushroom circle network
- Possibly the oldest active Rootbound guardian
- Can send players between sanctuaries as messengers

**Fire Born:**
- Neutral relationship - could trade potions for Flame Essence or information
- Brenner might seek her ancient knowledge about transformation

**Flame Seekers:**
- Could trade potions for rare ingredients they find on expeditions
- They might want to recruit her knowledge, which she'd decline

**Village in the Mist:**
- Unknown to Village residents (underground, isolated)
- Could become valuable trading partner if players broker introduction
- Her potions would be extremely valuable to Village population

**Mist Born:**
- Would oppose them (Rootbound protect sanctuaries from mist corruption)
- They might seek her ancient knowledge of the mist's origin

### Combat Notes

Naki avoids combat entirely - BFF protects her. If somehow forced into combat:

**Difficulty:** 8 (small, doesn't want to fight)
**Experiences:** Ancient Druid Magic +4, Potion Brewing +3, Bone Divination +2
**Thresholds:** 1 Major / 1 Severe (physically small and frail)

**Key Abilities:**
- **Acid Breath** - d8 magic damage at Very Close range (Drakona bloodline)
- **Transformation Magic** - Can shrink/enlarge herself or others temporarily
- **Marsh Call** - Summons small wetland creatures to create distractions

**Preferred Strategy:**
- Flee immediately, letting BFF handle combat
- Use her small size to escape through tiny passages in the cave
- Only fights if cornered AND BFF is incapacitated (extremely rare)

## Memorable Details

1. **Her bone-throwing set** contains dozens of Drakona teeth she's shed over centuries
2. **Glowing eyes** cast eerie green light across her potion-brewing equipment
3. **Cave smells** like herbs, mushrooms, and the faint acidic tang of her breath weapon
4. **She hums while brewing** - wordless, ancient melodies from before the mist
5. **BFF responds** to her third-person commands: "Naki's big friend, greet the visitors gently"
6. **Keeps a visitor log** - scratches names into cave wall, dozens of names from across the years
7. **Offers marsh tea** - tastes like pond water but clears 1 Stress (hospitality gesture)
8. **Her humpback** is covered with small mushroom growths - connection to the Mushroom Circle modifier
