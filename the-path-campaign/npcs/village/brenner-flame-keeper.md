# Brenner

**Faction:** Flame Seekers
**Location:** Sacred Flame Sanctuary / The Forge
**Role:** Village Flame Keeper and Master Smith
**Ancestry:** Giant

---

## Appearance
- Massive Giant, has to duck through most village doorways
- Faintly glowing flame-like lines reaching from hands to elbows on both arms
- Heavy leather work clothes and apron, well-worn from decades of use
- Calloused, scarred hands from years of smithing and flame-tending
- Tools always on belt (hammer, tongs, measuring tools)
- Dismissive when asked about the glowing lines - "Just forge burn scars that caught the light"

## Personality
- Gentle and soft-spoken despite massive size
- Practical and direct in communication - no wasted words
- Tracks Flame Essence reserves obsessively (village survival)
- Deeply grateful to serve the community that accepted them

## The Twist
Unknowingly transforming into Fire Born. The flame lines aren't scars - they're the first stage of becoming something more than human. Once the transformation completes, they'll awaken the ancient flame carrier buried beneath the village and gain control of it. They dismiss the changes as occupational hazards, unaware they're being called to bond with a dormant temple-construct.

## Goals & Motivations
- Serve the community that gave them purpose (primary duty)
- Keep the Sacred Flame burning
- Keep village equipment maintained (people's lives depend on it)

## What They Offer
- Sacred Rest (clear Mist Marks)
- Equipment repair
- Flame Canister crafting
- Custom weapons/armor using Sacred Flame heat
- Flame Essence status reports

## What They Want
- Village to thrive and be safe (primary concern)
- Flame Essence (means to keep village protected, not the end goal)
- Help with community projects and village needs
- PCs to contribute to the village, not just take from it

## Key Interaction Beats
- First meeting - at the forge, asks what PCs need and how they'll help the village
- Working while talking - never stops moving, always multitasking
- Checks on villagers regularly, knows everyone's needs
- Enjoys the craft but prioritizes urgent community needs over personal projects

## Secrets & Knowledge
- The flame lines have been spreading - started slow, now advancing noticeably

---

## GM Notes

**Fire Born Transformation Counter:** Start at 0. Increment by 1 each time PCs return from expedition.
- Early (1-7): Lines spread, faint glow near flame
- Middle (8-14): Pulled toward sanctuary, harder to leave
- Late (15-19): Clearly transforming, bound to brazier, glowing
- Final (20): Transformation completes

**Mist Born Response (Telegraphed):**
- Counter 10: Scouts at perimeter
- Counter 15: Organized patrols circle village
- Counter 18: Mist thickens, creatures mass
- Counter 20: Multi-wave assault as flame carrier awakens

**Outcome:** Gains control of flame carrier beneath village, settlement becomes mobile

**Campaign Significance:** First new Fire Born in centuries - major campaign milestone
