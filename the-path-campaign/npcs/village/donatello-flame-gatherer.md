# Donatello

**Faction:** Flame Seekers
**Location:** Flame Gatherer's Lodge
**Role:** Senior Flame Gatherer
**Ancestry:** Galapa

---

## Appearance
- Galapa with weathered, scarred shell
- Shell carved with tallies for every gatherer lost
- Covered in talismans and charms from the Path
- Carries expedition gear, always ready to go
- Moves surprisingly fast despite turtle appearance

## Personality
- Extremely superstitious - rituals before every expedition
- Gruff but protective of their teams
- Never leaves without proper preparations and good omens
- Collects lucky charms and protective talismans obsessively

## The Twist
One of their talismans grants mist immunity. If they're ever lost in the mist, they reappear at the village unharmed with no memory of how they got back. They don't know which talisman does it or that it even exists. Has happened multiple times - they attribute it to "luck" and "proper rituals." Great plot hook when PCs witness it or when the talisman is lost/stolen.

## Goals & Motivations
- Keep gatherer teams alive (every loss adds another tally)
- Perform proper rituals before expeditions (intense croaking, throwing mud on team members' faces for protection)
- Honor the dead by marking locations with cairns (also serves as navigation - shows which places have been visited)

## What They Offer
- Path condition reports and expedition planning
- Protective rituals (croaking ceremony, mud blessing)
- Navigation advice (knows cairn locations)
- Veteran survival tips
- Will lead dangerous expeditions if needed

## What They Want
- New talismans from the Path
- PCs to follow the rituals (gets upset if they refuse the mud blessing)
- Information about Path dangers
- Help building cairns at new locations

## Key Interaction Beats
- Refuses to let anyone leave without the croaking ceremony and mud blessing
- Gruff but caring - "You think you're ready? Fine. But you're getting the mud."
- Studies new talismans obsessively, adds them to collection
- Tells stories of lost gatherers while carving tallies
- Mock fights with PCs to test their readiness (ninja turtle style)

## Secrets & Knowledge
- Can divine the current Path configuration through rituals (croaking, talisman reading)

---

## GM Notes
- Use rituals to reveal Path information to players
- The mist immunity talisman is major plot hook (theft, loss, discovery)
- Mock fights can be combat tutorials or readiness tests
- Cairn-building is a nice feature to make the world more lived in
