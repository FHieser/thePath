# Nix

**Faction:** Factionless (Village Resident)
**Location:** Community Hearth
**Role:** Hearth Keeper
**Ancestry:** Goblin

---

## Appearance
- Small goblin constantly moving, never sits still
- Mismatched clothes, pockets stuffed with random items
- Somehow always has exactly what someone needs hidden somewhere on their person
- Wild hair/appearance but genuine warm smile

## Personality
- Fast talker, jumps between topics mid-sentence
- Finds humor in everything, makes people laugh even in dark times
- Chaotic organizing system only they understand ("It's right there! Next to the thing!")
- Genuinely cares about everyone, remembers details about people

## The Twist
Extremely curious - eavesdrops on everything, knows every village secret. People think the chaos means they're not paying attention. They're always paying attention.

## Goals & Motivations
- Learn everyone's stories and secrets
- Keep the community connected and laughing
- Make sure nobody eats alone

## What They Offer
- Hot meals and information (knows everyone's business)
- Connections between villagers
- Random useful items from chaotic pockets

## What They Want
- Gossip and news from the Path
- Help gathering materials when village needs supplies

## Key Interaction Beats
- Makes jokes during serious moments
- Pulls random items from pockets mid-conversation
- Creates gossip about the PCs between themselves ("Did you hear Player 1 was so drunk at the last party?" when nothing like that happened)
- Always appears exactly when PCs are having private conversations

## Secrets & Knowledge
- Knows every village secret from eavesdropping
- Overhears PC private conversations
- Knows who likes who, who's feuding, who's hiding something

---

## GM Notes
- Use them to spread rumors and connect story threads
- Comic relief character
- Information source (but info comes with chaos)
- Creates awkward situations by revealing things PCs thought were private
