# Letter from the Archon to Aerion von Moosgrund

*Found in the monastery — leads to the Meteorite Laboratory*

---

**To Aerion von Moosgrund**

If you found this, you have already seen my work in the cellar. Good. That saves me an explanation.

What is down there represents months of progress — crystalline material from these lands of the mist, shaped into something that moves, that responds, that *functions*. It is the furthest I have gotten with inorganic subjects. I am not dissatisfied with it.

But it is not what you are.

You carry something I cannot synthesize — a living system, already integrated, already running. The mutation in you did not kill you. It adapted. That distinction is everything, and it is the question I have been working toward since we spoke on the bridge.

The cellar was a starting point. My real laboratory is further in — near where the meteorite made landfall. That is where the work that matters is happening, and that is where I would like to continue our conversation.

These lands of the mist hold more than anyone could have imagined. Hidden structures, unknown materials, transformations no one has thought to study. I have no interest in exploring that alone when there is someone worth exploring it with.

Come find me at the impact site.

— *The Archon*
