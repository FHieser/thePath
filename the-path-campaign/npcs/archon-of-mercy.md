# The Archon of Mercy

**Faction:** None (works alone)
**Location:** Outpost (current), Meteorite Laboratory (primary workspace)
**Role:** Hemomancer alchemist, creator of Aerion, fallen war hero

---

## Appearance
- Human with self-inflicted modifications from experimentation
- Blue skin, long ears, long fingers
- Red cloak with embroidery
- Changes are the result of his own experiments

## Personality
- Clinical, detached—views living beings as raw material
- Obsessive focus on his work
- Speaks precisely, like documenting observations
- Cannot see his own monstrosity—believes his work is righteous

## The Twist
A legendary war healer who saved countless lives when conventional methods failed. As the war worsened, he turned to hemomancy—using the blood and bodies of the fallen to save the living. It worked. And for that, he was shunned.

Other healers called him abomination. Military command distanced themselves. The families of those whose corpses he "used" demanded justice. They gave him the title mockingly—"Archon of Mercy"—a sneer, like crowning a false king.

He accepted it. If they wanted a monster, he would be one.

## Theme in Relation to PCs
**Mirror to Aerion:** Both were transformed, both were rejected, both are obsessed. Aerion fights to stay himself; the Archon surrendered to his work. Aerion is what the Archon once was—the Archon is what Aerion could become.

**Hand in Power:** The Archon offers knowledge, improvement, control over the mutation—but always at terrible cost. He is the temptation to take the easy path.

**Fellow Explorer:** He's discovering the Starfall Kingdom at the same pace as the players. Not all-knowing—racing toward the same secrets. A competitor, not just a final boss.

## Goals & Motivations
- **What he wants:** To "heal and perfect" everyone—remake mortality itself
- **Why:** Recognition. Vindication. Proof that his methods were right all along.
- **The crystal plague:** A means to an end—unique material to advance his work
- **What he's willing to do:** Anything. The shunning freed him from limits.

## What They Offer
- **Improvement:** Making people "better"—stronger, more resilient, perfected
- **Healing:** His original purpose, now twisted—he still sees himself as a healer
- **Knowledge:** Understanding of the body, mutation, transformation
- **For Aerion specifically:** First in line. The Archon knows his work best—could stabilize, enhance, or explain what Aerion truly is

## What They Want
- **Knowledge:** Information about the crystal plague—he's still learning
- **Subjects:** "There is work to be done"—people to undergo his procedures
- **Materials:** Reagents from dangerous surrounding areas—he needs fetch work
- **Test objects:** Someone to test new formulas on—may promise enhancements in return
- **From Aerion:** His unique blood, data on independent mutation, cooperation

## Key Interaction Beats
- **The Switch:** Warm and engaged with those he sees as people—then instantly cold and clinical with test subjects. No transition. No hesitation.
- Values conversation with "underlings" and collaborators
- Makes no breaks when working—a subject is a subject, not a person
- The shift is jarring to witness

## Secrets & Knowledge
- Openly shares knowledge—raising understanding is part of his mission
- Will explain his methods, his research, his findings
- Does not hide what he does or why
- The only secrets are things he hasn't discovered yet
