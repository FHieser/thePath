# Copass

**Faction:** Brass Mechanicorum
**Location:** Highland Watch (elevated observation post)
**Role:** Scholar studying Path mechanics / Trader of ancient weapons

---

## Appearance
- Tarnished brass sphere with hunched sensor unit bristling with telescoping optical lenses
- Perched atop a two-legged walker construct at Highland Watch fortifications
- Lenses constantly click and whir as they track the shifting mist

## Theme in Relation to PCs
- **Knowledge Through Risk** - Copass's mist immunity and endless curiosity push PCs to venture into dangerous areas for valuable data
- Challenges the party to pursue understanding even when survival instincts scream to flee
- Offers rewards that tempt them deeper into the mist

## What They Want
- Detailed observations of Path configuration changes between sessions
- Mist behavior data from areas organics can access
- Organic emotional responses to mist exposure - data Copass cannot gather itself

## What They Offer
- Brass Mechanicorum gear traded for Path data
- Tips about the Underground network - particularly interested in pushing PCs to explore deep tunnels
- Plot hook: hints that at sufficient depth, the mist cannot reach (though other dangers await)

---

## GM Notes
- Speaks in short, mechanical bursts with clicking pauses between statements
- Never shows emotion but displays endless patience for detailed observations
- Use to introduce Underground as alternative to Sacred Flame sanctuaries
- "Mist can't reach deep enough" revelation should intrigue survival-focused parties
- Can provide connection to broader Brass Mechanicorum faction and their underground territories
- Potential recurring contact if PCs engage with Path research or Underground exploration
- May offer introduction to Mechanicorum settlements in exchange for exceptional data
