## Encounters

### Highland Watch (Outbound) - Copass Trading Post

Copass, a resourceful Ember Trader, has set up a temporary trading post at the ancient watchtower. He's using the elevated position to watch for other travelers and avoid mist patrols. He's well-stocked with useful equipment and eager to trade.Can 

**Copass - Ember Trader NPC:**
- Weathered human merchant with a practical demeanor
- Carries a heavily-modified backpack with expandable compartments
- Knows the shifting paths well and tracks stable locations
- Willing to share information in exchange for news or trade

**Available Trade Goods:**

*Loot (Reusable):*
- **Alistair's Torch** - Magic torch with large illumination radius that never requires fuel
- **Fire Jar** - Instantly produces fire, regenerates on long rest (perfect for lighting signal fires or starting campfires)
- **Skeleton Key** - Grants Advantage on Finesse Rolls to unlock doors and mechanisms
- **Arcane Prism** - Provides +1 bonus to Spellcast Rolls for allies within Close range
- **Glider** - Deploy while falling to control descent (costs a Stress to use)
- **Homing Compasses (Pair)** - Two compasses that always point toward each other (sold as set)

*Consumables:*
- **Minor Health Potion** - Clear 1d4 HP (limit 5 per character)
- **Minor Stamina Potion** - Clear 1d4 Stress (limit 5 per character)
- **Grindeltooth Venom** - Add d6 to next physical weapon damage roll
- **Vial of Darksmoke** - Roll d6s equal to Agility, add highest to Evasion vs one attack
- **Sweet Moss** - Consume during rest to clear 1d4 HP or 1d4 Stress

*Mundane Supplies:*
- **Premium Rations** - Preserved food suitable for Path travel
- **Basic Healing Supplies** - Bandages, salves, and medicine

**Information Exchange:**
Copass shares valuable knowledge if players engage with him:
- **Underground Hint**: "At some level of depth the mist can't reach... *wink* Dangerous though. I've heard Stone Works has mine shafts that go deep."
- **Path Stability**: Can explain how some locations remain stable while others shift with the mist
- **Mist Patterns**: Warns that the mist has been more active lately, especially around locations with strong light sources
