# Quests

Definition:
- Name: A short name for the quest
- Questgiver: The NPC that gives the quest
- Description: A short description of the quest
- Reward: The reward for completing the quest
- Steps: The steps to complete the quest
  - Step 1: The first step
  - Step 2: The second step
  - Step N: The Nth step
