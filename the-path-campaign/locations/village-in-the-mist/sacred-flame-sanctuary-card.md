# Sacred Flame Sanctuary

***Tier 1 Social***
*The summit of the village hill, where the eternal Sacred Flame burns alongside a watchtower, providing protection and hope.*
**Impulses:** Maintain the sacred flame, watch for dangers and opportunities, provide sanctuary for the weary

> **Difficulty:** 11
> **Potential Adversaries:** Mist Creatures (if flame fails)

## FEATURES

***Sacred Cleansing - Passive:*** When PCs rest in the village, they automatically cleanse 1d4 + Tier Mist tokens without spending a downtime move. The village flame's exceptional power and the community's accumulated cleansing knowledge make this the safest sanctuary. PCs still choose their normal two downtime moves.

  *What rituals cleanse the mist's corruption from your body and spirit? How does the village community support your recovery?*

***Flame Crisis - Reaction:*** When Flame Essence runs critically low, the GM gains 2 Fear as panic spreads through the village and the Sacred Flame's protective power weakens.

  *How does the village react to the dimming flame? What desperate measures do people consider?*

***Creatures in the Mist - Action:*** Spend 3 Fear to have Mist creatures breach the village's protective barrier and attack directly. This creates a challenging encounter as the creatures threaten residents and sacred spaces.

  *What types of creatures emerge from the Mist? How do villagers react when their sanctuary is violated?*

***Sanctuary Breach - Reaction:*** When the Sacred Flame is extinguished, start a Breach Counter at 1. Each dawn while the flame remains out, all PCs gain Mist Marks equal to the current counter value, then increase the counter by 1. The counter is set to 0 when the flame is relit.

  *How does the creeping mist corruption manifest in the village? What signs show the protection failing?*

## FEATURE QUESTIONS

- What prayers or ceremonies do people perform at the Sacred Flame?
- How does the flame's light affect the behavior of Mist creatures?
- What would happen if someone tried to take flame directly from the brazier?
- How do different people experience the flame's presence differently?
