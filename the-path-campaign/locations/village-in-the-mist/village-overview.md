# Village in the Mist

## Core Concept

The **Village in the Mist** serves as the central sanctuary and starting point for all Path journeys. This settlement exists as a stable location protected by an ancient Sacred Flame, providing refuge for travelers, wanderers, and those seeking safety from the otherworldly Mist.

---

## **Location Description**

### **Physical Layout**
The village is built on terraced levels ascending a natural hill:

- **Summit**: **Sacred Flame Sanctuary** - The eternal Sacred Flame burns alongside a watchtower, providing protection and hope
- **Upper Terrace**: **Village Forge** - Smithy using Sacred Flame heat for equipment crafting and repair
- **Middle Terrace**: **Community Hearth** - Social center where residents gather for meals, news, and stories
- **Lower Terrace**: **Flame Gatherer's Lodge** - Rough quarters where expedition teams plan dangerous flame-gathering missions
- **Base Level**: **Supply Cache** - Storage areas and project coordination center for village improvements
- **Connecting Paths**: Switchback trails and stone steps between levels

### **The Sacred Flame**
- **Ancient Bronze Brazier**: Ornate vessel containing the eternal flame
- **Protective Radius**: Creates a wide circle of clear air, pushing back the Mist
- **Visible Beacon**: Can be seen from great distances through the Mist
- **Mystical Properties**: Provides warmth, light, and supernatural protection

### **Architecture & Atmosphere**
- **Rough Construction**: Practical buildings focused on survival over aesthetics
- **Mixed Materials**: Stone retaining walls, wood construction, makeshift repairs
- **Defensive Features**: Watchtower next to Sacred Flame provides observation
- **Forge Integration**: Smithy positioned to use Sacred Flame's radiant heat
- **Terraced Gardens**: Small growing areas on different levels
- **Drainage Systems**: Stone channels direct water down the hillside

## **Sacred Flame Resource System**

### **Resource Mechanics**
**Sacred Flame Resources** represent the mystical fuel required to maintain the village's protection.

#### **Resource Type: Flame Essence**
- **Physical Form**: Crystallized fragments of sacred fire found at ancient sites
- **Properties**: Glows with inner warmth, dissolves when added to Sacred Flame
- **Rarity**: Found only at locations with connection to pre-Mist civilization
- **Identification**: Recognizable by experienced Path walkers and flame keepers

#### **Consumption Rate**
- **Daily Consumption**: Village consumes 1 Flame Essence per day
- **Critical Timeline**: Creates constant survival pressure
- **Population Effects**: Additional residents increase daily consumption
- **Weather Influence**: Mist storms may increase consumption rate
- **Activity Impact**: Heavy Path traffic requires more protection

#### **Gathering Methods**
Players acquire Flame Essence through dangerous expeditions:
- **Early Path Sources**: Initial Path configurations always include fire sources
- **Ancient Ruins**: Primary source found at pre-Mist sacred sites
- **Risk/Reward**: GM gains Fear tokens when players collect flame essence
- **Mist Opposition**: The Mist actively works against Sacred Flame
- **Daily Expeditions**: Flame Gatherers venture out almost daily for survival
- **Special Canisters**: Required for transport, created by village forge

#### **Storage & Management**
- **Limited Storage**: Village can store maximum 3 units of Flame Essence (3-day buffer)
- **Physical Storage**: Secure vault in forge area near Sacred Flame
- **Monitoring**: Flame Keeper/Smith tracks reserves and consumption daily
- **No Emergency Reserves**: Upgrades required to increase storage capacity
- **Transport Canisters**: Forge creates special containers for flame transport

#### **Failure States**
When the Sacred Flame weakens or fails, the village faces escalating consequences:
- **Flame Crisis**: When Essence runs critically low, the GM gains 2 Fear as panic spreads through the village
- **Sanctuary Breach**: When the Sacred Flame is extinguished, a Breach Counter starts at 1. Each dawn while the flame remains out, all PCs gain Mist Marks equal to the current counter value, then the counter increases by 1. The counter is set to 0 when the flame is relit
- **Mist Creature Incursions**: The GM can spend 3 Fear to have Mist creatures breach the village's protective barrier and attack directly
- **Infrastructure Breakdown**: Village projects and improvements begin failing without flame protection
- **Escalating Corruption**: The longer the flame remains out, the more dangerous the village becomes

## **Village Service Economy**

### **Essential Services** *(Always Available)*

#### **Rest & Recovery**
- **Sanctuary Rest**: Full recovery of HP, Stress, and conditions
- **Sacred Flame Healing**: Enhanced recovery compared to normal rest
- **Safe Storage**: Secure locations for equipment and valuables
- **Basic Shelter**: Simple accommodations for travelers

#### **Basic Supplies**
- **Food & Water**: Essential provisions for Path journeys
- **Simple Equipment**: Basic tools, rope, simple weapons
- **Repair Services**: Mending torn gear and damaged equipment
- **Path Preparations**: Maps, compass, basic survival gear

### **Information Exchange**
Information flows naturally through service interactions:
- **Traveler Tales**: Stories and rumors from recent Path expeditions
- **Location Reports**: Current conditions at known Path destinations
- **Danger Warnings**: Alerts about Mist storms, hostile creatures, or unstable areas
- **Trade Opportunities**: News of resources, allies, or beneficial exchanges

### **Service Quality & Relationships**
Village services are affected by the party's standing with residents:

#### **Individual NPC Relationships**
- **Personal Favors**: Specific NPCs may ask for specifc services or information
- **Story Connections**: Personal NPC storylines that develop over time
- **Conflict Resolution**: Repairing damaged relationships through actions

### **Payment Systems**
Services can be paid for through:
- **Standard Currency**: Coin when available
- **Labor Exchange**: Helping with village tasks or community projects
- **Information**: Valuable news, maps, or knowledge from Path exploration

## **Village Population**

### **Core Residents** *(Essential NPCs)*

#### **The Flame Keeper** *(Sacred Flame Sanctuary)*
- **Role**: Maintains Sacred Flame and manages Essence reserves
- **Services**: Sacred Rest for clearing Mist Marks, flame tending guidance, watchtower vigil coordination
- **Knowledge**: Ancient flame lore, sacred site locations, flame crisis management
- **Personality**: Wise, protective, deeply concerned with village safety

#### **The Smith** *(Village Forge)*
- **Role**: Equipment crafting and repair using Sacred Flame heat
- **Services**: Weapon/armor creation, equipment repair, flame canister crafting
- **Knowledge**: Metalworking techniques, equipment durability, specialized crafting
- **Personality**: Skilled artisan, practical, takes pride in quality work

#### **Senior Flame Gatherer** *(Flame Gatherer's Lodge)*
- **Role**: Coordinates dangerous expedition teams and survival knowledge
- **Services**: Path condition reports, veteran's advice, emergency expedition planning
- **Knowledge**: Path dangers, Mist activity, expedition survival techniques
- **Personality**: Weathered explorer, protective of team members, honors the fallen

#### **The Hearth Keeper** *(Community Hearth)*
- **Role**: Maintains community bonds and village morale
- **Services**: Communal meals, story coordination, crisis counseling
- **Knowledge**: Village relationships, community needs, conflict resolution
- **Personality**: Warm, welcoming, natural mediator who brings people together

### **General Population**
- **Refugees**: Recent arrivals seeking safety from the Mist
- **Wanderers**: Experienced travelers who chose to settle temporarily
- **Families**: Long-term residents building new lives in the sanctuary
- **Specialists**: Craftspeople, scholars, healers who provide essential skills
- **Path Veterans**: Retired explorers with extensive knowledge and experience

### **Population Dynamics**
- **Arrival Rate**: New refugees appear periodically needing assistance
- **Departure Rate**: Some residents leave to search for other sanctuaries
- **Community Building**: Relationships form and evolve between residents
- **Resource Strain**: Larger population increases Flame Essence consumption

## **Village Project System**

The village can be expanded through ongoing improvement projects coordinated at the **Supply Cache**. These include both community-driven village projects that advance automatically each day, and PC-driven Path security projects that enhance travel safety. See the Supply Cache location card for detailed project mechanics and examples.

## **Village Evolution**

### **Long-term Development**
- **Population Growth**: Successful management attracts new residents
- **Infrastructure Expansion**: Prosperity allows for upgraded facilities
- **Regional Influence**: Well-established village becomes known throughout Path network
- **Cultural Development**: Unique traditions and customs emerge from community decisions

### **Crisis Management**
- **Flame Shortage**: Emergency measures when Essence runs low
- **Population Pressure**: Managing resources when too many refugees arrive
- **External Threats**: Defending against Mist creatures or hostile human groups
- **Internal Conflicts**: Resolving disputes between different resident factions

*The Village in the Mist serves as both sanctuary and strategic resource, requiring ongoing player investment while providing essential support for Path exploration.*
