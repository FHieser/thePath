# THE BROOD THRONE

***Tier 2 Exploration***
*A vast underground chamber where the Spider Queen rules from her throne of woven silk, surrounded by countless egg sacs and the wealth of a hundred plundered caravans.*
**Impulses:** Protect the Queen and her brood, test intruders' worthiness, preserve the colony's future

> **Difficulty:** 14
> **Modifiers:** Unstable, Underground, Wild Beasts
> **Potential Adversaries:** Spider Queen (Solo/Bruiser), Elite Guardian Spiders, Spider Swarms, Silk Constructs

## FEATURES

***The Queen's Fortress - Passive:*** The Silken Spire and the Brood Throne beneath it exist as a single unified domain, always appearing together when this location card is drawn during Path generation. The fortress manifests at the edge of the Path as a side branch - a dead-end location that does not connect onward to other Path locations. PCs can see the gleaming white silk towers from the main Path and choose whether to investigate this optional detour. Both locations share the same Unstable modifier state - if one is stabilized using Flame Essence, both become Stable. The Cave Mouth modifier on the Silken Spire represents the silk-wrapped descent shaft that spirals down through the earth to the Brood Throne chamber below. PCs must clear the surface fortress before they can safely access the underground lair.

  *How visible is the Silken Spire from the main Path? What draws travelers to investigate this side branch? Does the Spider Queen deliberately position her fortress where it can be seen, or does she prefer secrecy?*

***Silk Strand Descent - Traversal:*** At the heart of the Silken Spire, a vertical shaft descends deep into the earth, its walls lined with tightly woven silk cables that spiral downward into darkness. This is the Cave Mouth connection to the Brood Throne below. PCs must climb down thick silk ropes to descend approximately 100 feet to reach the underground throne chamber. The descent requires a group Agility roll. On a success, the group safely reaches the Brood Throne chamber below. On a failure, one PC (players choose) slips on the silk and falls partway down, taking **2d6+3 physical damage** and becoming separated from the group - they arrive in a different section of the Brood Throne. On a failure with Fear, the PC's fall sends vibrations through the silk that alert the Spider Queen to their arrival, and she prepares an ambush. PCs can ascend the same way to return to the surface.

  *How deep does the shaft descend before reaching the underground chamber? What sounds echo up from the Brood Throne below? Can the spiders cut the silk ropes to trap intruders below or prevent pursuit?*
