# Initial Path - Location Ideas

*Collection of location concepts for the first Path configuration from Village in the Mist*

## Location Development Methodology

### **Step 1: Generate Individual Cool Locations**
- Brainstorm standalone location concepts with evocative names and one-line descriptions
- Focus on atmospheric, memorable places that spark imagination
- Don't worry about categories or themes yet - just collect interesting ideas

### **Step 2: Group Locations into Pairs or Thirds**
- Look for natural thematic connections between individual locations
- Create groups of 2-3 locations that work well together
- Consider spatial relationships (how locations connect or relate to each other)
- Name each group with a thematic title

### **Step 3: Iterate to Reach 3+ Subthemes per Location**
- Go through each group systematically
- Add third locations to pairs where needed
- Ensure each group has at least 3 distinct subthemes/locations
- Maintain thematic coherence while adding variety

### **Key Benefits of This Approach:**
- **Creative first**: Start with cool ideas before worrying about structure
- **Natural groupings**: Let themes emerge organically from the locations themselves
- **Systematic completion**: Ensure every location group is fully developed
- **Flexible iteration**: Easy to adjust and refine as you work through each group

### **Location Types:**
Currently developed: **Surface Locations** and **Underground Locations**

Future location types could include: Water/River Locations, Deep Forest Locations, Mountain Locations, Sea Locations, Swamp Locations, etc.

---

**Surface Locations:**

- **Windswept Grasslands**
    - Open Fields: Strong winds always sweep over the grass covered fields.
    - Hillside Shrine: A small altar built into a grassy slope, offerings long since scattered.
    - Windmill Ruins: The crumbling remains of an old windmill on a hilltop, its blades long since fallen.

- **Sacred Mountain Pass**
    - Mountain Path: A treacherous way through the mountains. Don't fall down.
    - Flame Shrine: An ancient shrine where a Sacred Flame flickers weakly in the eternal mist.
    - Mountain Peak: A windswept summit where ancient ceremonies were once held under open sky.

- **Forest Refuge**
    - Hut under the Oak: A home built under a huge oak. Who lives here?
    - Dark Grove: A dense forest with towering trees and shadowed undergrowth.
    - Mushroom Ring: A perfect circle of massive toadstools in a forest glade.

- **Misty Waters**
    - Fog Lake: A still lake perpetually shrouded in thick mist, its depths unknown.
    - Boathouse + Dock: A weathered pier extending into murky waters with a half-collapsed structure where old vessels slowly rot away.
    - Drowned Village: Submerged houses where glowing roots wind through broken walls and empty doorways.

- **Ancient Crossing**
    - Old Bridge: An ancient bridge spanning over a cliff waiting to crumble. Once it breaks down, what waits in the dark?
    - Broken Statue: The shattered remains of a monument to someone long forgotten.
    - Broken Stairs: Crumbling stone steps that descend into the depths below the bridge.

- **Stone Works**
    - Abandoned Quarry: Ancient stone excavation site with exposed cliff faces and hidden caves.
    - Cliff Face: A sheer rock wall with narrow ledges and hidden alcoves.
    - Crystal Caverns: Underground chambers filled with glowing crystals that hum with ancient energy.

- **Overgrown Ruins**
    - Overgrown Temple: A crumbling shrine consumed by thorns and creeping vines.
    - Overgrown Cemetery: Ancient graves reclaimed by wild vines and twisted trees.
    - Overgrown Garden: Wild plants reclaiming formal flower beds and pathways.

- **Trading Grounds**
    - Trading Post: The skeletal remains of what was once a crossroads merchant station.
    - Old Well: A deep stone well with a bucket that hasn't been drawn in years.
    - Watchtower: A tall stone tower overlooking the crossroads for spotting approaching threats.

- **Highland Watch**
    - Rocky Outcrop: A cluster of large boulders jutting from the earth like ancient teeth.
    - Watchtower: An abandoned stone tower overlooking the surrounding landscape.
    - Signal Fire: Ancient stone platform where beacons once warned of distant dangers.
    - Stone Cairns: Stacked rock markers left by previous travelers and scouts.

**Underground Locations:**

- **Fungal Depths**
    - Mushroom Cavern: A vast underground space where giant fungi create their own ecosystem.
    - Bone Piles: Something is down here and it is hungry.
    - Root Chamber: Underground space where massive tree roots have broken through the ceiling.

- **Flooded Network**
    - Underground Lake: A dark body of water fed by hidden springs in the depths.
    - Flooded Tunnels: Water streams coming from somewhere. Maybe they lead to the surface?
    - Water Wheel: Ancient brass and iron machinery still turning with the underground current, powering unknown mechanisms.

- **Geothermal Caverns**
    - Lava Tubes: Tunnels carved by molten rock, now cool but still radiating heat.
    - Heat Vents: Hot gas pushing out.
    - Heat Engine: Ancient bronze machinery that harnesses the thermal vents to power mysterious underground mechanisms.

- **Sacred Underground**
    - Huge Cavern: A massive empty space where your voice echoes endlessly into the darkness.
    - Stone Pillars: Giant columns of stone that hold up the cavern ceiling.
    - Pillar Shrine: A sacred altar built around a natural formation where the cavern floor dips into a bowl shape.

- **Mining Complex**
    - Central Hub: There still is a lot of mining gear lying around.
    - Ancient Door: Somebody wanted to keep something or some out.
    - Mining Cart Rails: Old tracks that once carried ore to the surface.



### Location Modifiers
*Optional overlays that transform base locations*

- Mist-Touched: Location corrupted by prolonged mist exposure - adds Mist Walkers, reality distortions, and unstable terrain.
- Resource-Rich: Location contains specific materials the village currently needs (timber, stone, herbs, etc.) - GM adapts based on current village projects.
- Inhabited: Location has NPCs present - travelers, refugees, traders, or other communities.
- Cave Mouth: The entrance to a deep cave system carved into a rocky hillside - adds underground access to any location.
