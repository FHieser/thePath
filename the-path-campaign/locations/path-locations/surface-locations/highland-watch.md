# HIGHLAND WATCH

***Tier 1 Traversal***
*A cluster of ancient stone formations where watchtowers and signal fires once formed a defensive line against creatures emerging from the mist.*
**Impulses:** Maintain vigilance against mist incursions, provide elevated sanctuary, preserve anti-mist defenses

> **Difficulty:** 11
> **Potential Adversaries:** Mist Walkers, Highland Predators (Mist-touched variants), Corrupted Stone Guardians

## FEATURES

***Rocky Outcrop Fortification - Passive:***

***Watchtower Vantage - Passive:*** The ancient stone tower provides commanding views of the surrounding landscape. PCs who climb to the top can survey the area and gain a general description of one of the nearby environments currently connected to this location. With careful observation, they can identify modifications present in the surrounding environments (such as Mist-Touched, Resource-Rich, Inhabited, etc.). The elevated position reveals what ground-level travel cannot.

***Signal Fire Ward - Action:*** 

  *What fuel keeps the protective flames burning? How long do the wards remain active? What happens when the fire dies and mist creatures surround the position?*

***Cairn Bindings - Passive:*** Each time PCs build a cairn at this highland position, mark progress on a counter (start at 0). When the counter reaches 10, the accumulated stone markers create a powerful navigational anchor and Highland Watch gains the **Stable** modifier. If the counter drops below 10 for any reason, the location reverts to **Unstable**. The cairns hold ancient power to anchor locations against the shifting mist.

  *What ancient power flows through these carefully stacked stones? How do the cairns communicate with each other across the mist? What happens when travelers disturb or add to the sacred stone arrangements?*

## FEATURE QUESTIONS

- What ancient disaster first brought the mist and its creatures to threaten this highland region?
- How effective were these defensive positions against the supernatural mist incursions?
- What knowledge about fighting mist creatures was preserved in the watchtower records?
- Could these highland defenses be restored to protect against current mist threats?
