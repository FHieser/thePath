# MISTY WATERS

***Tier 1 Exploration***
*A fog-shrouded lake where drowned ruins emerge from dark waters and abandoned structures decay along the shore.*
**Impulses:** Conceal dangerous depths, preserve drowned memories, lure the unwary to watery graves

> **Difficulty:** 11
> **Potential Adversaries:** Aquatic Beasts (Giant Pike, Bog Shambler), Drowned Spirits, Mist Phantoms

## FEATURES

***Fog-Shrouded Depths - Passive:*** The lake's thick mist limits visibility to Close range. PCs attempting to navigate the water or identify distant objects have disadvantage on Instinct rolls. - What ancient tragedy caused an entire village to vanish beneath these dark waters?
- How do the glowing roots connect to the drowned structures below?
- What valuable knowledge or treasures might be recovered from the sunken buildings?
- Why does the mist here seem thicker and more persistent than in other areas?
