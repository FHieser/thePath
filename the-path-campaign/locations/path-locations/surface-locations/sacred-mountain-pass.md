# SACRED MOUNTAIN PASS

***Tier 1 Traversal***
*A treacherous mountain path leading to ancient ceremonial grounds where a Sacred Flame flickers against the mist.*
**Impulses:** Challenge travelers with dangerous terrain, preserve ancient sacred traditions, guard the flame's mysteries

> **Difficulty:** 11
> **Modifiers:** Stable
> **Potential Adversaries:** Mountain Beasts (Giant Eagle, Stone Bear), Flame Guardians, Mist-touched Pilgrims

## FEATURES

***Treacherous Path - Passive:*** The mountain path is narrow and unstable. When moving along the path, PCs must succeed on an Agility roll or mark 1 Stress from a near-fall. On a failure with Fear, they lose their footing and must be rescued or fall Close range down the mountainside.

  *What makes this particular section so dangerous? Are there signs of previous accidents? How do experienced mountain travelers navigate safely?*

***Sacred Flame Blessing - Passive:*** At the Flame Shrine, PCs can spend time in reverent contemplation of the flickering Sacred Flame. Make a Presence roll. On a success, clear 2 Stress. On a success with Hope, also gain advantage on the next roll made to resist fear or despair.

  *How does the Sacred Flame respond to each PC? What visions or feelings does the flame inspire? Why does this flame burn when others have been extinguished?*

***Mountain Winds - Action:*** Fierce winds howl across the peak during ceremonies. All creatures on the mountain summit must succeed on a Strength Reaction Roll or be pushed Very Close range toward the cliff edge. Creatures already at the edge must make an additional Agility roll to avoid falling.

  *From which direction do the winds come? Do they carry voices or other sounds? How did ancient ceremonies account for these dangerous winds?*

***Ancient Echoes - Reaction:*** When PCs perform any ritual or ceremony at the mountain peak, the stones echo with memories of past ceremonies. Spend a Fear to manifest ghostly figures of ancient worshippers who either aid (+2 to the ceremony roll) or interfere (disadvantage) based on the PCs' reverence.

  *What ceremonies were once performed here? Who were the ancient worshippers? What do the echoes reveal about the mountain's history?*

## FEATURE QUESTIONS

- Why does this Sacred Flame burn when so many others have been extinguished by the mist?
- What ancient ceremonies gave this mountain pass its sacred significance?
- How do the treacherous paths serve to test the worthiness of pilgrims?
- What powerful beings or forces might still guard the sacred sites here?
