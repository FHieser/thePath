# VILLAGE IN THE MIST

**_Stable Settlement_**
_A fortified village protected by a Sacred Flame, serving as the primary safe haven for travelers navigating the mist-shrouded lands. Stone walls encircle modest dwellings, workshops, and communal spaces where survivors gather to share stories and prepare for their journeys into the unknown._
**Impulses:** Shelter the weary, preserve community, maintain the Sacred Flame

> **Difficulty:** —
> **Modifiers:** Stable, Flame-Touched, Inhabited

## CENTRAL HUB

The village serves as the primary staging point for all Path expeditions. The Sacred Flame at its heart keeps the corrupting mist at bay—this location always remains Stable and cannot disappear between sessions. Mist adversaries cannot enter the village, and resting here does not grant Mist tokens. Characters can cleanse accumulated corruption: Short Rest removes 1d4 + Tier Mist tokens, Long Rest removes ALL Mist tokens. The village contains essential facilities including a forge, community hearth, supply cache, and Flame Gatherer's Lodge where NPCs offer trade, quests, and information about the surrounding lands.

_Every expedition into the mist begins and ends here. The village is both sanctuary and staging ground._

## FEATURE QUESTIONS

- What brought the original settlers to establish this village at this precise location?
- How does the community decide who ventures into the mist and who stays to tend the flame?
- What happens if the Sacred Flame's essence drops too low? Has it ever gone out?
- Who tends the Sacred Flame, and what ancient knowledge do they guard?
- Are there other villages like this one surviving somewhere in the mist?
