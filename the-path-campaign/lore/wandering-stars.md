# The Wandering Stars

_Twenty-five planets visible above the mist, each governing a domain. Travelers who know their names learn to ask the right questions._

---

| Planet | Domain | Appearance |
|---|---|---|
| **The Red One** | Danger | Deep crimson, almost blood-colored. Pulses faintly on clear nights, as though breathing. |
| **The Pale One** | Hope | Soft white-silver, gentle and steady. Looks like a distant candle that refuses to go out. |
| **The Near One** | Connection | Bright blue-white, unnervingly large for a star. Appears closer than it should, as though it is watching back. |
| **The Hidden One** | Change | Dim, faintly green. Rarely visible unless you already know where to look. Most people mistake it for nothing at all. |
| **The Bright One** | Truth | Piercing white, almost painful to look at directly. Leaves faint afterimages. The only planet that casts a shadow. |
| **The Twin** | Choice | Two small bodies orbiting each other, inseparable. One is always slightly brighter than the other, though which one shifts from night to night. |
| **The Wanderer** | Deception | Shifts between yellow and deep orange. Its position is unreliable — it appears where you don't expect it, and vanishes where you do. |
| **The Dead One** | Loss | Barely visible, a flickering gray-blue, like an ember nearly out. Some nights it disappears entirely. |
| **The Still One** | Stability | Deep violet, fixed. Unlike every other body in the sky, it never moves — not with the seasons, not with the hours. A reliable north. |

### Time

| Planet | Domain | Appearance |
|---|---|---|
| **The Leaving One** | The Past | Dim and gray, drifting steadily westward across the sky. As it nears the edge it briefly blazes — a pale flash — then vanishes. It does not set. It simply leaves. |
| **The Little One** | The Present | A tiny pale body orbiting the center of the sky in a slow, unchanging circle. Never seems to go anywhere, yet never stops moving. |
| **The Coming One** | The Future | Blue-white, entering from the eastern edge with a brief blaze of light before settling into the sky. Whether it approaches or merely passes through, it never stays long. |

### Alignment

| Planet | Domain | Appearance |
|---|---|---|
| **The Warm One** | Friend | A small amber-gold pinpoint, usually quiet. Every few minutes it flares — a brief cross of warm light — then returns to its gentle glow as though nothing happened. |
| **The Cold One** | Foe | Pale blue and small, easy to overlook. It flares periodically in a sharp cross of cold light, precise and brief, then goes still again. |
| **The Even One** | Neutral | An undistinguished gray pinpoint. Flares periodically with colorless light — not warm, not cold — then fades back to nothing. |

### Conflict

| Planet | Domain | Appearance |
|---|---|---|
| **The Driving One** | Force | A streak of deep orange that appears suddenly at the edge of the sky and burns toward the center before fading. It does not drift. It charges. |
| **The Gentle One** | Mercy | Soft green, low in the sky. Easy to miss. Its glow expands and contracts slowly, like breathing. Those who find it tend to lower their voices. |
| **The Receding One** | Retreat | A blue-gray streak that tears diagonally across the sky toward the far corner, fading as it goes. Always moving away. Never looking back. |

### Fortune

| Planet | Domain | Appearance |
|---|---|---|
| **The Rising One** | Good Fortune | Bright yellow-gold, drifting visibly upward through the night. Once it passes above the mist line it disappears — then starts again from below. |
| **The Falling One** | Misfortune | A burnt orange body descending steadily as though weighted. It falls until it disappears below the horizon, then returns to fall again. |
| **The Circling One** | Unknown Fortune | Pale violet, looping slowly in a wide arc. It always returns to where it started, which tells you something. |

### Knowledge

| Planet | Domain | Appearance |
|---|---|---|
| **The Clear One** | The Seen | Brilliant near-white with a faint luminous ring hovering around it at a fixed distance. Everything nearby looks dimmer by comparison. |
| **The Whispered One** | The Heard | So faint it might not be there at all. A barely visible ring circles it at a distance — just enough to confirm something is present. Look directly and both vanish. |

### Singular

| Planet | Domain | Appearance |
|---|---|---|
| **The Shrouded One** | Mist | Pale and haze-covered, its edges lost in a diffuse glow. Small wisps of cloud drift slowly across its face, briefly obscuring it before passing on. |
| **The Burning One** | Flame | A tight cluster of shifting light — orange, red, briefly amber at its hottest — that breathes and flickers like something consuming itself. Not a single body. Several. |
