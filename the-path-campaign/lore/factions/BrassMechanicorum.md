# Brass Mechanicorum

## Physical Form
**All Brass Mechanicorum share a distinctive spherical body design:**
- Central brass sphere serves as the core housing (contains mechanisms, consciousness, power source)
- Optics and mouth pieces attach as a separate unit on top/front of the sphere
- This separate sensor unit creates a characteristic hunched forward appearance
- Arms and legs attach directly to the sphere via articulated joints
- The forward-leaning optics unit gives them an inquisitive, observing posture
- Size varies from small (1-2 feet diameter) to massive (10+ feet diameter)
- Surface features include: optical lenses, tool ports, measurement instruments, inscription panels
- Limb configuration varies by function (bipedal walkers, quadrupedal haulers, multi-armed craftsmen)

## Mist Protection
**Mechanical Immunity:**
- Brass Mechanicorum are unaffected by mist corruption (non-organic composition)
- Can traverse deep mist zones that would kill organic beings
- This immunity allows unprecedented mist research and mapping
- However, prolonged mist exposure causes mechanical degradation (corrosion, precision loss)

## Item Identity
- **Theme:** Precision-engineered devices built from brass, gears, and crystalline lenses. Effects tend toward **Utility** and **Protection** - measuring, analyzing, revealing hidden information, or shielding the bearer through mechanical means. Items feel clinical and exact - they click, whir, and calibrate. They work reliably regardless of mist or flame, but require maintenance and can break down.
- **Typical Cost:** Mechanical degradation - items have limited uses before needing repair or recalibration. Alternatively, they may require specific components or materials to fuel.
