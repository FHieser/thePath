# Mist Born

## Themes
- Guardians of THE Nothing - three primordial void-spheres at the lowest depths chain the sleeping god of War and Expansion
- Descend into gigantic underwater caverns where Ancient Guardians (crocodile, kraken, crab) watch over absolute void
- Broken mortals taught by divine constructs and spawned beings budding from the living mist-organism
- Follow inverse spirituality: enlightenment through descent and fusion with the void, not ascent and separation
- Caught in expansion crisis as THE Nothing operates unbalanced without Expansion to oppose it, causing mist to consume the mortal realm
- Sea + Tibet aesthetics: diving suits with prayer inscriptions, underwater monasteries, coral-void fusion, meditation into living emptiness

## History
- Imprisoning gods took primordial void and used it to chain the god of War and Expansion in unconscious sleep
- Created Ancient Guardians (giant divine constructs) to watch over THE Nothing's three void-spheres
- First mortals wandered into the mist and descended into the depths, gazing upon THE Nothing
- "If you gaze into the abyss, the abyss gazes back" - mortals were broken by the void
- Ancient Guardians took them in and taught them: the god must sleep to prevent catastrophic divine war
- Broken mortals established underwater monasteries and began fusion with the living mist-organism
- The organism began budding new Mist Born (The Broken) from consumed souls
- THE Nothing's expansion accelerates as the god's domain of Expansion sleeps, leaving void unopposed
- Imprisoning gods departed and do not return to address the crisis

## General Goals
- Maintain eternal watch over THE Nothing's three void-spheres (primary duty)
- Prevent Fire Born from reaching the depths and destroying the chains
- Guard against the god's awakening to prevent catastrophic divine war
- Descend deeper into fusion with the mist-organism (spiritual progression for mortals)
- Extinguish Fire Born flames to prevent their spread and navigation

## Housing & Architecture
- Underwater monasteries built into cavern walls beneath the sea of mist, descending toward THE Nothing
- Diving bell meditation chambers suspended at various depths
- Coral-void structures - organism grows through and around architecture
- Ancient Guardian "temples" - massive alcoves where the divine beasts rest
- No surface structures - Mist Born who venture up use temporary camps or corrupted ruins

## Territories & Areas
- Gigantic underwater caverns beneath the sea of mist, descending to THE Nothing's three void-spheres
- Three void-spheres located at different points across the realm (locations shift due to mist's divine realm properties)
- Deepest territories at the lowest points where void-spheres anchor reality
- Underwater monasteries clustered along descent routes at various depths
- Surface presence in Mist-Touched locations and corrupted areas - temporary occupation only

## Mist Protection
- They ARE the mist - fused with the organism, no protection needed
- Early-stage mortals still wear diving suits inscribed with prayers
- Progressive fusion grants immunity - coral-void growths act as living protection
- Cannot survive outside the mist for extended periods (opposite of Fire Born)
- Advanced fusion stages require mist presence to sustain themselves
- Ancient Guardians are uncorrupted divine constructs - immune to mist but also don't need protection

## Subgroups

### Ancient Guardians
- Giant divine constructs (crocodile, kraken, crab)
- Watch over THE Nothing's three void-spheres
- Teach mortals who descend deep enough to find them
- Uncorrupted and eternal

### First Disciples
- Original mortals taught directly by Ancient Guardians
- Maintain underwater monasteries
- Preserve Guardian teachings in purest form
- Most knowledgeable about cosmological balance

### The Broken
- Lost souls that succumb to the mist
- Losing themselves to the void
- May be shaped into mist constructs
- Take on shapes to combat the fires of the Fire Born

## Relationships with Other Factions

### Flame Aligned
**Fire Born**: Hostile - Attack on sight. Nothing does not care, therefore Mist Born only see their purpose. No hatred, just indifferent elimination of those trying to free the god.

**Flame Seekers**: Hostile - They carry flame. Extinguish flames, eliminate threats to the mist.

### Neutral
**Ember Traders**: Hostile - They transport flames between sanctuaries and map the Path, aiding Fire Born navigation. Priority targets.

**Factionless**: Indifferent - Only relevant if they carry flame or threaten the depths.

**The Rootbound**: Hostile when Rootbound threatens - Natural sanctuaries resist mist without explanation. Ancient beings outside Mist Born's understanding. Attack if they interfere with the mist's purpose.

### Mist Aligned
**Mist Clergy**: Allied - Share belief that imprisonment must be maintained. Clergy uses persuasion and politics; Mist Born enforce through violence. Complementary approaches.

**Mist Born**: N/A (self)

### Underground
**Deep Dwellers**: Indifferent - Underground communities where mist doesn't reach. Separate realms, no conflict.

**Brass Mechanicorum**: Indifferent - Ancient machines with unclear purpose. Not relevant to guarding the void unless they interfere.

## Rumors
*(What Flame Seekers and other mortals whisper about the Mist Born)*

- "Stay away from the deep caverns beneath the mist. Things down there that aren't human anymore. Coral growing through their skin."
- "Saw one once, crawling out of the fog. Didn't walk right. Didn't breathe right. Just... stared. When I ran, it didn't follow. Like I wasn't worth the effort."
- "They say the mist makes its own soldiers. People who get lost become... something else. Part of it."
- "Don't look into the deep waters beneath the mist. There are shapes down there. Big ones. They've been watching since before we were born."

## Plot Hooks
- **Monk to the Surface**: One of the First Disciples rises from the depths seeking something on the surface. They are not hostile to players and invite them to help. What does a disciple of the Ancient Guardians need from the world above?
- **The Village Gets Attacked**: Movement in the mist. The Broken attack a flame sanctuary in numbers never seen before. What provoked this sudden aggression?
- **Finding a Void Sphere**: Players discover a massive tunnel descending into the true depths beneath the sea of mist. Something vast lurks there - an Ancient Guardian, or worse. What do they do with this knowledge?
- **The Mist Parts**: The mist suddenly recedes, revealing a completely new area with unfamiliar locations, dangers, and aesthetics. Are there rules governing the mist's movements? Subareas leading to different realms? What does this reveal about the divine field's nature?
