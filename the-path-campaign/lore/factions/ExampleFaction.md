## Workflow: How to Create a Faction

When creating a new faction, work through each section one by one in this order:

1. **Themes** - Establish core identity and what makes them interesting. Add a twist that distinguishes them from generic fantasy factions.

2. **History** - Define origin, major transformative events, and how they came to be what they are now.

3. **General Goals** - What do they want? What drives them? Keep it focused and tied to themes.

4. **Housing & Architecture** - How do they physically exist in the world? What do their structures look like and why?

5. **Territories & Areas** - Where do they live and operate? What environments suit them?

6. **Mist Protection** - How do they survive the mist's influence? What are their methods' strengths and weaknesses?

7. **Subgroups** - Internal divisions based on function, philosophy, or role. Usually 3-4 distinct groups.

8. **Relationships with Other Factions** - Go through all other factions. Consider: Allied/Neutral/Hostile and why.

9. **Rumors** - What do common people say about this faction? Keep it mysterious and grounded in observable details.

10. **Plot Hooks** - 4 concrete adventure seeds that bring players into contact with this faction in different ways.

**Important**: Go through each section collaboratively, discussing and refining before moving to the next. The twist you add to each faction makes them memorable.
