# Islands in the sea of mist

- To create a manageable Section of different locations it makes sense to group them based on the individual background story they might had before they moved into the sea of mist.
- that allows for a packaded grouping were these cards can come together under a central theme to make the discovery a uniform idea.

- Giant Walls defending a dying place

  - Endless wall through the fog with a closed gatehouse
  - **Border Post** - Checkpoint where guards turned back their own people to contain the plague
  - **Message Tower** - Rookery where birds are still housing
  - **Breach Point** - Collapsed section where zombie siege engines rot amid scattered bones of both sides
  - **Refugee's Hope** - Camp at the lordship's edge where families waited for entry that never came

- A plague that ran through the ruling land of a lordship

  - Lost villages with mass graves and undead roaming
    - **Plague Warren** - Abandoned quarantine village where doors are nailed shut from the outside and something still scratches from within
    - **The Charnel Pit** - Mass grave that exhales cold breath and draws carrion birds that never land, only circle endlessly
    - **Ashen Sepulcher** - Collapsed tomb half-buried in volcanic ash, with passages that lead far deeper than the structure should allow
  - A city district that is barred

    - **Sealed District** - City quarter barricaded from both sides, scratches on the inside of every door
    - **Pest House** - Hospital where the dying were sent
    - **Physician's Folly** - Doctor's manor filled with desperate experimental cures and preserved specimens
    - **The Burning Fields** - Scorched land where they tried to burn the sickness away, ash still warm

  - Last Stand
    - **Scaffold Square** - Execution ground where justice became mob rule in the final days
    - **Sanctuary Keep** - Barricaded temple where survivors made their last stand against plague and panic
    - **Riot Market** - Trading plaza destroyed when order collapsed, stalls overturned and looted

- And old castle

  - Top Level
    - **Lord's Observatory** - Tower where the last lord watched his domain die through expensive telescopes
    - **Scribes' Archive** - Library documenting the lordship's final days in increasingly frantic handwriting
  - Basement
    - **The Last Ball** - Palace ballroom where aristocrats danced while the world burned outside
    - **Armory Hollow** - Weapon racks stripped bare, marks on walls where desperate hands grabbed anything sharp
    - **Empty Courthouse** - Halls of justice abandoned mid-trial, accused still chained to empty benches
  - Dungeon
    - **Forgotten Oubliette** - Secret dungeon cells below the dungeon, prisoners who were left to starve in darkness
  - Hidden temple

- Lost locations that are not as corrupted
  - Rootbound Location
