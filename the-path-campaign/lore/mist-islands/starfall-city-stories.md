## Story Threads

Each thread is a chain of chimes (and in some cases physical evidence) that ends in a reward.

---

**The Love Story** *(chimes)*
- Starts as one thread — establishes the soldier and nurse as a couple
- **Splits** at the moment the soldier comes to the nurse for the last time and leaves
- **Nurse's thread** → follows her work, grief, and peace → ends at Sanctuary Keep (2,3)
- **Soldier's thread** → follows his rage and regret → ends at the castle gate
- *Intersects with:* Security Chief at 1,2 (the orders), Noble at 1,3 (the burning)

**The Mechanic** *(shortest — runs through robots directly)*
- Players follow robot sightings and behavior — the robots ARE the thread
- Chimes supplement but robots lead
- Ends at Workshop (3,1) with the brass bird
- *Reward:* item or robot ability

**The Noble**
- Traces his dissolution district by district
- **Shares a moment with the Nurse** at 1,3 — a chance meeting at the university hospital at the start of the plague. Neither knows yet what they'll become.
- Ends at University/Burning Fields (1,3)
- *Reward:* item

**The Merchant** *(most extensive — mostly physical evidence, minimal chimes)*
- Ledgers, notes, bodies — an investigation trail more than a memory trail
- Already mapped as the treasure trail
- Ends at the Vault
- *Reward:* items + access

**The Security Chief**
- Traces his fear becoming control becoming nothing — ends as the Warden
- *Intersects with:* Love Story + Noble at 1,1 (the decree), Love Story at 2,2 (the orders)
- Ends at the top of the Council Hall tower (1,1) — The Fall chime
- **The Warden's Last Memory** plays on death — released from his core, not a found chime
- *Reward:* defeating the Warden grants access to wherever he was guarding

---

## Scene Rulings

- Each chime does **one thing**: lands an emotional note OR reveals a connection OR reframes something
- Each chime **points forward** — players always know there is more to find
- **2-3 chimes per district**, Workshop and Sanctuary slightly more
- Players who explore everything get the **full picture** — no permanent gaps
- Threads **intersect** but don't merge — shared moments are seen from different angles

## District Assignments

### 1,1 — Council Hall
*Threads: Security Chief, Noble (intersection — the decree)*

**Location notes:**
- Large council chamber with a table, many seats — council members, clerks
- Window in the corridor outside overlooking Scaffold Square (1,2)
- Has a tower — requires climbing to reach the top
- The roof is entirely covered in crystal — where he fell, where he transformed
- The Warden spawns at the base of the tower — this is where the Security Chief fell and transformed

---

### The Decree
**District:** 1,1 — Council Hall
**Thread:** Security Chief + Noble intersection
**Characters:** Security Chief, Noble, council members, clerks
**Camera:** POV — through the Security Chief's eyes

- A crowded chamber. Faces around the table, all looking at him.
- A decree on the table in front of him. Quarantine orders. His name at the bottom line.
- He picks up the quill. Sets it down.
- The Noble reaches across from his seat. Takes the quill. Signs without reading it.
- Slides it to the next council member. Already talking, already moving to the next order.
- The Security Chief watches his own unsigned name get passed around the table.
- He stands. Steps out into the corridor.
- A window. The scaffold in the square below, empty and waiting.
- He stares at it.

**Points toward:** 1,2 — Scaffold Square
**Pointer:** His gaze through the window — the scaffold visible below
**Placement:** Hanging above the council table

---

### The Fall
**District:** 1,1 — Council Hall (tower)
**Thread:** Security Chief (final)
**Characters:** Security Chief
**Camera:** POV — through his eyes, then black

- Stairs. Each step slower than the last. His hand on the wall leaving glowing smears.
- The top. Wind. The city burning below — the university, the market, all of it.
- He breathes out. Crystals catch the light in the air.
- The city tilts.
- Black.

**Points toward:** Nothing — end of his thread. Players look down from the tower and see where he landed.
**Pointer:** None
**Placement:** Top of the Council Hall tower — requires climbing

---

### The Warden's Last Memory
**District:** 1,1 — Council Hall (released on death)
**Thread:** Security Chief (end — plays when the Warden is defeated)
**Characters:** Security Chief
**Camera:** POV — flashes from black, each one brief

- The roof. Close. Blood spreading across crystal.
- Black.
- His hands. Crystal crawling up from the wrists.
- Black.
- Other bodies. His arms pulling them in. They don't resist.
- Black.
- The streets. Larger now. Each step cracking stone.
- Black.
- Mist rolling in across the city. Everything going quiet.
- Black.
- The players. Looking up at him. Weapons raised.
- Black.

**Points toward:** Nothing — end of thread
**Pointer:** None
**Placement:** Released from the Warden's core on death — not a found chime

### The Reading
**District:** 1,2 — Scaffold Square
**Thread:** Security Chief
**Characters:** Security Chief, guards, crowd
**Camera:** POV — through his eyes

- The scaffold steps. A crowd below, packed tight.
- He reads from the decree. His voice steady. The crowd is not.
- Something connects with his head. He stops reading.
- His hand goes up. Comes away wet with blood.
- Guards rushing in from the sides, batons raised.
- Beyond them, through the chaos — the market. Stalls overturning. Already spreading.

**Points toward:** 2,2 — Riot Market
**Pointer:** The market visible through the chaos
**Placement:** Hanging on the scaffold post

### 1,3 — University / Burning Fields
*Threads: Noble, Love Story (intersection — the burning), Nurse (intersection — her parents)*

**Location notes:**

### The Introduction
**District:** 1,3 — University / Burning Fields
**Thread:** The Love Story (Nurse)
**Characters:** The Nurse, The Soldier, her parents
**Camera:** Medium — parents in the foreground, she watches from behind

- A wide stone corridor, tall windows, afternoon light across the floors.
- Her parents stand together in a doorway — white coats, unhurried, at home here.
- The soldier fills the corridor awkwardly. Too much uniform, not enough books.
- Her father extends a hand. The soldier takes it carefully, like something breakable.
- A bell rings somewhere above. The corridor fills with movement — the day ending.
- She catches his eye over her father's shoulder.

**Points toward:** Next Love Story chime
**Pointer:** None — establishes the university and the relationship /@todo look over this again
**Placement:** Hanging in the main entrance corridor

---

### The Blue Light
**District:** 1,3 — University / Burning Fields
**Thread:** The Noble + Nurse intersection
**Characters:** The Noble, the parents
**Camera:** Slow pull-back — he fills the frame at first, gradually gets smaller

- A ward room. The parents stand either side of a bed, curtain half-drawn.
- They gesture toward something behind the curtain. A blue shimmer leaks through the fabric.
- The Noble steps closer. Looks. Steps back.
- The parents watch him calmly. This is already their normal.
- A crystal-encrusted hand reaches through the curtain and draws it closed. The bell rings.

**Points toward:** Noble's thread — his dissolution begins here
**Pointer:** None — emotional beat, establishes the plague's first visible sign /@todo look over this again
**Placement:** Hanging near the ward entrance, same corridor as The Introduction

### The Orders
**District:** 2,2 — Riot Market
**Thread:** Security Chief + Love Story intersection
**Characters:** Security Chief, guards, the Soldier, crystallized dead
**Camera:** POV — through his eyes

- The market in ruins. Overturned stalls, broken glass, guards dragging people away.
- He walks through it. Blood on his temple still drying.
- Something moves between the stalls. A crystallized figure lurches upright — sprays shards as it swings.
- The ground again. His hands shaking against the cobblestones.
- He stands. Looks at his hands — crystal dust on both of them.
- He points. Two guards toward the Sealed District. Go.
- He turns. The soldier is there, watching him.
- He points at the soldier. Then toward the university.

**Points toward:** 1,3 — University/Burning Fields (soldier's thread), 3,3 — Sealed District (Security Chief's thread)
**Pointer:** His pointing finger — two directions, two consequences
**Placement:** Hanging from an overturned market stall frame

### Sneaking In
**District:** 3,3 — Sealed District
**Thread:** The Love Story (Nurse — opening)
**Characters:** The Nurse, her friends
**Camera:** Medium — friends in foreground, she's behind them

- A street at night. The university towers over the far end, lit from within.
- A group of young women hurry past, laughing. She's last, slightly apart.
- They reach a pub door. The friends spill inside.
- She looks back once at the university. Then follows them in.

**Pointer:** Players remember the tower. At 1,3 — it's gone.
**Placement:** Hanging in the doorframe of the pub entrance

### The Dance
**District:** 3,3 — Sealed District
**Thread:** The Love Story (Soldier — opening)
**Characters:** The Soldier, The Nurse
**Camera:** Wide establishing, slow push-in toward his face

- A crowded pub. A soldier at the bar, watching.
- He watches the nurse across the room — not the crowd, her.
- He crosses. Says something. She laughs.
- They dance. He's bad at it.
- Push-in lands on his face. Young. Certain.

**Pointer:** None — establishes the couple.
**Placement:** Hanging above the bar inside the pub
