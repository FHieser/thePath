# Starfall Crystal System

## Acquisition Guidelines

- **Standard Encounters**: Minor crystal loot as part of rewards
- **Going Out of Their Way**: Extra crystals for taking risks, exploring dormant areas
- **Temptation Rewards**: Visible crystals in dangerous or morally weighted situations
- **Special Enemies**: Crystallized champions and leaders carry more
- **Harvesting**: Describe the action, reward engagement—no special rolls needed

## Consumables

- **Crystal of Might**: Press against a weapon before combat. For the scene, reroll up to 3 damage dice per attack. Repeated use risks cracking the weapon.
- **Crystal Arrows**: Arrowheads, bolts, or sling stones carved from crystals. +d6 magic damage on hit, target glows and loses concealment. Shatters on impact.
- **Ember Dust**: Crystal ground with Sacred Flame ash. Scatter over a weapon to choose physical or magic damage for the scene.
- **Starfall Grenade**: Brittle shell packed with crystal shards. On impact, all creatures in Very Close range mark 1 Stress.
- **Dormancy Paste**: Thick paste applied to flame canisters. Prevents crystallized dead from awakening for your stay in a location—even with Sacred Flame present.

---

## Enchantments/Runes

- **Shard Inlay**: Embed crystals into a weapon's edge. When you roll max on a damage die, the target marks 1 Stress.
- **Glassbone Plating**: Crystal shards fused to armor joints. Once per rest, reduce severity of a hit by one threshold (Severe → Major → Minor).
- **Plague Whisper**: Crystal dust worked into weapon bindings. Gain advantage when searching for crystallized creatures or structures.
- **Dormancy Rune**: Carved crystal sigil worn on the body. Once per session, force a crystallized creature to select a different target instead of you.

---

## Weapons & Armor

Crystals can be used to forge tier-appropriate weapons and armor with added features.

### Weapon Features

- **Reliable**: +1 to attack rolls
- **Returning**: When thrown within range, appears in your hand immediately after the attack
- **Versatile**: Weapon can also be used with alternate statistics
- **Massive**: −1 to Evasion; on a successful attack, roll an additional damage die and discard the lowest
- **Scary**: On a successful attack, the target marks 1 Stress

### Armor Features

- **Reinforced**: When you mark your last Armor Slot, increase your damage thresholds by +2 until you clear at least 1 Armor Slot
- **Quiet**: +2 to rolls to move silently
- **Gilded**: +1 to Presence
- **Flexible**: +1 to Evasion

## Magic Items

- **Resonance Lantern**: Lantern with a crystal core instead of flame. Sheds cold light that doesn't awaken crystallized dead.
- **The Last King's Ring**: Band set with a meteorite sliver. Once per session, reroll any roll—but you hear distant thunder.
- **Starfall Compass**: A crystal shard suspended in liquid. Always points toward the nearest concentration of crystals.
- **Crystal Charm**: Tiny crystal on a chain. Once per rest, reduce incoming magic damage by half.

---

## Village Upgrades

- **Crystal Greenhouse**: Growing structure lined with crystals. Plants grow faster—players heal 1 HP automatically when resting at the village.
- **Starfall Forge**: Specialized forge capable of working crystal into weapons and armor.
- **Pathfinder's Shrine**: Map table with embedded crystal markers. Makes predictions more reliable. Dice size halves, but add half the dice size to automatically discovered locations (d6 → d3 + 3 predictions).
- **Crystal Farm**: Cultivated crystal growths tended by villagers. Produces 1d3 shards every day.
