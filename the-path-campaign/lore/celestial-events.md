# Celestial Phenomena and Their Significance

## Moon Phases

**Blood Moon** (total lunar eclipse)
- Strengthens transformations
- Weakens magic (or vice versa)

**Blue Moon**
- Peaceful event
- Fighting / bloodshed strictly forbidden

**Full Moon**
- Peak of energy

**New Moon**
- New beginnings / starts / hope

---

## Rare Events

**Solar Eclipse**
- Certain potion / spell ingredients must be harvested at this exact moment, etc.

**Meteor Shower**
- Dark omen
- Meteor fragments important for spells

**Parade** (min. 5 planets in a row)

**Conjunction** (very close together)
- Symbol of connection (Death / Life / Past / Future / Present)

**Comets**
- Various comets, passing by planets at different intervals
- Every 75 / 500 years → cyclical, permanence, great energy
