# Mist Stalker

**Tier 1 Skulk**

*A more agile and cunning manifestation of the mist's hunger, this creature moves with predatory grace through the otherworldly fog. Its form shifts between solid and ethereal, allowing it to strike from unexpected angles before vanishing back into the mist.*

**Motives and Tactics:** Ambush from mist, strike and fade, hunt light sources, isolate targets

**Difficulty:** 11
**Thresholds:** 4/8
**HP:** 3
**Stress:** 2
**ATK:** +1 **Ethereal Claws:** Melee — 4 phy/mag
**Experience:** Heat Sense +2, Stealth +3

## Features

### Heat Seekers — Passive
They are the hounds sent out by the mist to seek out flames and extinguish them. Mist Stalkers prioritize fire, light sources, and warm-blooded creatures when selecting targets.

### Mist Step Strike — Action
**Mark a Stress** to have the Stalker move to any point within Far range, ignoring obstacles and enemies by briefly becoming incorporeal. Immediately after moving, make an attack against a target within Melee range.

### Chilling Presence — Reaction
When this adversary makes a successful attack against a PC, you gain a Fear.

---

*These were once scouts, rangers, or rogues who knew the hidden paths before The Path consumed the world. Now their skills serve the mist, using their knowledge of stealth and movement to hunt those who carry the light they once protected.*
