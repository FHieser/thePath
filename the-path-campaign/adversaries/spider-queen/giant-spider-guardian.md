# Giant Spider Guardian

**Tier 2 Bruiser**

*A horse-sized arachnid with glistening black chitin and legs thick as tree branches. These elite hunters serve as the Queen's personal guards, resting in the Sentinel Chamber between patrols and striking with terrifying coordination when threats arise.*

**Motives and Tactics:** Protect the Queen, ambush from above, overwhelm prey, guard silk fortress

**Difficulty:** 15
**Thresholds:** 12/26
**HP:** 6
**Stress:** 5
**ATK:** +3 **Crushing Fangs:** Very Close — 2d10+4 phy
**Experience:** Ambush +3, Climb +2

## Features

### Frenzy Strike — Action
**Spend a Fear** when spotlighted to make the Guardian's standard attack against all targets within range.

### Web Anchor — Action
**Mark a Stress** to make an attack against a target within Close range. On a success, deal 2d8 physical damage and the target becomes Restrained in silk cables until they break free with a successful Strength or Agility Roll.

### Ceiling Ambush — Reaction
When the Guardian drops from above onto a target (from ceiling or higher position), they deal an additional 1d8 physical damage and the target becomes Vulnerable until the end of their next turn as they're knocked off balance by the impact.

### Momentum — Reaction
When the Guardian makes a successful attack against a PC, you gain a Fear.
