# Example Encounters

A comprehensive guide to building balanced encounters in Daggerheart for The Path campaign.

---

## **Battle Points System**

### **Base Formula**

**Battle Points (BP) = 3 × (number of PCs) + 2**

| Party Size | Base BP |
|------------|---------|
| 2 PCs | 8 BP |
| 3 PCs | 11 BP |
| 4 PCs | 14 BP |
| 5 PCs | 17 BP |
| 6 PCs | 20 BP |

### **Encounter Modifiers**

Apply these modifiers to the base BP calculation:

| Modifier | BP Adjustment |
|----------|---------------|
| Easier or shorter fight | -1 |
| Includes 2 or more Solos | -2 |
| Add 1d4 (or +2) to adversary damage rolls | -2 |
| Deploy an adversary from a lower tier | +1 |
| No Bruisers, Hordes, Leaders, or Solos | +1 |
| Harder or longer fight | +2 |

**Note:** Reserve 1-2 BP for environmental features/hazards in complex locations.

---

## **Adversary Costs**

| Adversary Type | Battle Points |
|----------------|---------------|
| Minions (equal to number of PCs) | 1 |
| Social or Support | 1 |
| Horde, Ranged, Skulk, or Standard | 2 |
| Leader | 3 |
| Bruiser | 4 |
| Solo | 5 |

## **Encounter Scaling Examples**

### **Example 1: Mist Horde Assault**

**Theme:** Overwhelming swarm led by corrupted commander

| Party | Composition | Total |
|-------|-------------|-------|
| **2 PCs** | 1 Wraith Leader (3) + 2 Stalkers (4) + 4 Walkers (1) | 8 BP |
| **3 PCs** | 1 Wraith Leader (3) + 3 Stalkers (6) + 6 Walkers (2) | 11 BP |
| **4 PCs** | 1 Wraith Leader (3) + 4 Stalkers (8) + 12 Walkers (3) | 14 BP |
| **5 PCs** | 1 Wraith Leader (3) + 6 Stalkers (12) + 10 Walkers (2) | 17 BP |
| **6 PCs** | 2 Wraith Leaders (6) + 6 Stalkers (12) + 6 Walkers (2) | 20 BP |

**Tactics:** Wraith Leader uses Rally the Lost to activate walkers in waves. Stalkers use Mist Step Strike for hit-and-run attacks from fog. With 6 PCs, dual Leaders coordinate devastating combined assaults.

---

### **Example 2: Mounted Assault**

**Theme:** Bruiser platform with support rider creating corruption zones

| Party | Composition | Total |
|-------|-------------|-------|
| **2 PCs** | 1 Crab (4) + 3 Throwers (3) + 2 Walkers (1) | 8 BP |
| **3 PCs** | 2 Crabs (8) + 3 Throwers (3) | 11 BP |
| **4 PCs** | 2 Crabs (8) + 3 Throwers (3) + 1 Stalker (2) + 2 Walkers (1) | 14 BP |
| **5 PCs** | 2 Crabs (8) + 3 Throwers (3) + 3 Stalkers (6) | 17 BP |
| **6 PCs** | 3 Crabs (12) + 4 Throwers (4) + 2 Stalkers (4) | 20 BP |

**Tactics:** Throwers ride crabs for +2 Evasion, use Spread the Mist to create corruption zones. Crabs grab PCs with Pincer Grasp. Stalkers ambush from fog. Walkers swarm flame-carriers. With multiple mounted pairs, they can control different zones of the battlefield.

---

### **Example 3: Coordinated Strike**

**Theme:** Tactical team with commander, tank, and zone control

| Party | Composition | Total |
|-------|-------------|-------|
| **2 PCs** | 1 Wraith Leader (3) + 3 Throwers (3) + 4 Walkers (2) | 8 BP |
| **3 PCs** | 1 Wraith Leader (3) + 1 Crab (4) + 2 Throwers (2) + 1 Stalker (2) | 11 BP |
| **4 PCs** | 1 Wraith Leader (3) + 1 Crab (4) + 3 Throwers (3) + 2 Stalkers (4) | 14 BP |
| **5 PCs** | 1 Wraith Leader (3) + 2 Crabs (8) + 2 Throwers (2) + 2 Stalkers (4) | 17 BP |
| **6 PCs** | 2 Wraith Leaders (6) + 2 Crabs (8) + 2 Throwers (2) + 2 Stalkers (4) | 20 BP |

**Tactics:** Wraith Leader coordinates attacks using Rally. Crabs lock down frontline PCs with Pincer Grasp. Throwers create mist zones for battlefield control. Stalkers exploit confusion and hunt isolated targets. With larger parties, multiple Leaders and Crabs create devastating tactical control.

### **Example 4: Jagged Knife Ambush**

**Theme:** Organized criminals using terrain and teamwork

**Jagged Knife Gang (Tier 1):**
- **Jagged Knife Lieutenant** - Leader (3 BP) - Commands gang, tactical coordination
- **Jagged Knife Kneebreaker** - Bruiser (4 BP) - Heavy damage dealer, locks down targets
- **Jagged Knife Sniper** - Ranged (2 BP) - Ranged attacks from cover
- **Jagged Knife Bandit** - Standard (2 BP) - Frontline fighter (Tier 3)
- **Jagged Knife Shadow** - Skulk (2 BP) - Backstab specialist
- **Jagged Knife Lackey** - Minion (1 BP for matching # of PCs) - Overwhelming numbers

| Party | Composition | Total |
|-------|-------------|-------|
| **2 PCs** | 1 Lieutenant (3) + 2 Snipers (4) + 2 Lackeys (1) | 8 BP |
| **3 PCs** | 1 Lieutenant (3) + 1 Kneebreaker (4) + 2 Snipers (4) | 11 BP |
| **4 PCs** | 1 Lieutenant (3) + 1 Kneebreaker (4) + 3 Snipers (6) + 2 Lackeys (1) | 14 BP |
| **5 PCs** | 1 Lieutenant (3) + 1 Kneebreaker (4) + 2 Bandits (4) + 3 Snipers (6) | 17 BP |
| **6 PCs** | 1 Lieutenant (3) + 1 Kneebreaker (4) + 1 Shadow (2) + 2 Bandits (4) + 3 Snipers (6) + 6 Lackeys (1) | 20 BP |

**Tactics:** Lieutenant commands from advantageous position using Tactician and Coup de Grace. Kneebreaker engages frontline fighters with Hold Them Down. Snipers use Unseen Strike from cover targeting spellcasters. Bandits use Climber and From Above for vertical advantage. Shadow uses Backstab and Cloaked for assassination attempts. Lackeys swarm isolated targets.
