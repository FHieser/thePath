# BFF (BIG FUCKING FROG)

**_Tier 2 Solo_**  
_An enormous amphibian the size of a wagon, covered in mottled green and brown skin that glistens with toxic slime. Bioluminescent patterns pulse across its warty hide, mesmerizing and deadly._  
**Motives & Tactics:** Lure prey with glowing patterns, dissolve armor with caustic secretions, spray acid at groups, devour intruders.

> **Difficulty:** 14  
> **Thresholds:** 12/24  
> **HP:** 8  
> **Stress:** 4  
> **Attack:** +3 | **Toxic Bite:** Melee | 2d8+6 magic damage

**Experience:** Devour +2, Spread Toxins +2

---

## FEATURES

**_Relentless (2) - Passive:_** This adversary can be spotlighted up to two times per GM turn. Spend Fear as usual to spotlight them.

**_Toxic Slime - Passive:_** When BFF makes a successful attack, the target must mark an Armor Slot without receiving its benefits (they can still use armor to reduce the damage). If they can't mark an Armor Slot, they must mark an additional HP.

_The frog's toxic secretions corrode metal and leather, eating through protective gear._

**_Light Lure - Action:_** Spend a Fear to have BFF's bioluminescent patterns glow with an enticing light. All targets within Close range must succeed on an Instinct Reaction Roll or be Lured in until they mark 2 Stress or take damage. Targets that are Lured in must move within Melee range of BFF.

_The hypnotic patterns pulse with otherworldly beauty, drawing victims inexorably closer._

**_Acid Spray - Action:_** Spend a Fear to make an attack against all targets in front of BFF within Close range. Targets BFF succeeds against take 2d10+2 magic damage and must mark an Armor Slot without receiving its benefits (they can still use armor to reduce the damage). If they can't mark an Armor Slot, they must mark an additional HP and you gain a Fear.

_The massive frog expels a spray of caustic digestive fluid, sizzling as it melts through armor and flesh._

**_Momentum - Reaction:_** When BFF makes a successful attack against a PC, you gain a Fear.

_Each successful strike empowers the beast, making it more dangerous with every moment._
