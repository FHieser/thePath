# EELIGATOR

**_Tier 2 Solo_**
_A massive predator combining the worst traits of electric eel and alligator - twelve feet of scaled muscle with webbed claws and a wickedly barbed tail. Its hide crackles with bio-electric energy, and its jaws lock with bone-crushing force._
**Motives & Tactics:** Ambush prey from submerged positions, drag victims underwater to drown, defend the black water lake from intruders.

> **Difficulty:** 14
> **Thresholds:** 12/25
> **HP:** 7
> **Stress:** 5
> **Attack:** +3 | **Snapping Jaws:** Very Close | 2d10 physical damage

**Experience:** No Escape +3

---

## FEATURES

**_Relentless (2) - Passive:_** This adversary can be spotlighted up to two times per GM turn. Spend Fear as usual to spotlight them.

**_Move Through Mire - Passive:_** While within its hunting grounds (the black water), any effect that might slow or limit the Eeligator's movement is ignored. With its webbed feet and low center of gravity, the Eeligator is perfectly suited to navigate water and unstable terrain.

_The creature glides effortlessly through the viscous black water, where others would struggle with every step._

**_Healing Skin - Passive:_** At the start of each turn, the Eeligator may mark a Stress to heal 1 HP.

_The beast's regenerative flesh knits wounds shut almost as quickly as they're made._

**_Death Grip - Action:_** When you hit with a Snapping Jaws attack, you can mark a Stress to lock the target in its jaws. The target is Restrained until an action is used to escape or the Eeligator takes Major damage. Each turn, the Eeligator pulls the target closer to the water, causing the target to mark a Stress.

_The powerful jaws clamp down like a vice, dragging the struggling victim inexorably toward the dark water._

**_Pull Under - Action:_** If the Eeligator starts its turn within Very Close range of the black water with a target under the effects of Death Grip, spend 1 Fear to drag them under, making them Restrained and Drowning. While Drowning, at the end of each turn the target is spotlighted, they mark a Stress.

_With a violent thrash, the predator vanishes beneath the surface, taking its prey with it into the suffocating depths._

**_Volt Strike - Action:_** Spend 2 Fear to attack a target with the Eeligator's electrically charged tail. On a success, deal 2d10 magical damage and the target becomes Vulnerable.

_The barbed tail whips forward with a crack of electricity, stunning its victim and leaving them exposed._

**_Ambushing Grab - Action:_** If a creature is within Very Close range while the Eeligator is submerged in water, spend 3 Fear to take the Death Grip action without marking Stress. If the attack hits, you may immediately use the Pull Under action, spending Fear to do so as normal.

_The water explodes as the hidden predator erupts from below, jaws already clamping down on its unsuspecting prey._

**_Momentum - Reaction:_** When the Eeligator makes a successful attack against a PC, you gain a Fear.

_Each successful strike empowers the beast, making it more dangerous with every moment._
