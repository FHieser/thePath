# BLOOD FERN PREDATOR

**_Tier 2 Solo_**  
_A massive wolf whose fur has been overtaken by spreading crimson moss that grows like living blood, eyes gleaming with predatory intelligence._  
**Motives & Tactics:** Stalk intruders through fern cover, ambush from elevated positions, drag prey into the valley depths, mark territory with killed deer.

> **Difficulty:** 15  
> **Thresholds:** 13/25  
> **HP:** 9  
> **Stress:** 4  
> **Attack:** +3 | **Moss-Slick Bite:** Melee | 2d10+4 physical damage

**Experience:** Valley Hunter +3

---

## FEATURES

**_Relentless (2) - Passive:_** This adversary can be spotlighted up to two times per GM turn. Spend Fear as usual to spotlight them.

**_Fern Stalk - Action:_** Mark a Stress to vanish into the waist-high blood ferns and reposition to any location within Far range that has fern cover. Immediately make a Melee attack with Advantage.

_The predator melts into the crimson undergrowth, only to explode from the ferns elsewhere._

**_Territorial Roar - Action:_** Uses: 1

All PCs within Far range must make a Presence Reaction Roll (15). Those who fail cannot move closer to the predator until end of their next turn and mark 1 Stress.

_Its roar echoes through the valley, a primal warning that freezes prey in their tracks._

**_Moss-Covered Hide - Passive:_** Uses: 1

The thick blood moss coating protects the predator. Reduce the severity of all physical damage by one step (Severe becomes Major, Major becomes Minor, minimum of Minor). The first time the predator would take Severe physical damage, apply the reduction (Severe becomes Major), then this ability is lost - the moss armor is shattered and provides no further protection.

_Blades and arrows sink harmlessly into the dense moss growth - until one devastating blow tears through, shattering the protective layer entirely._

**_Valley's Bond - Passive:_** The predator is the valley's collector, manifestation of ancient pacts. If killed, its body collapses and dissolves into spreading blood moss. The predator is permanently defeated for this session. However, if the party returns to Blood Fern Valley in any future session, the valley reforms its collector from the crimson ferns (full HP, all abilities restored). The debt stones hang as reminder - debts must always be paid, and guardians must always enforce them.

_You can drive it back, but never truly destroy it. The valley will not forget its collector._
