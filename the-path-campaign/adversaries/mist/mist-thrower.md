# Mist Thrower

**Tier 1 Support**

*A hunched figure encased in a crude diving suit of salvaged metal and leather, its faceplate fogged with otherworldly mist. It carries a repurposed apparatus that spews corrupting fog, spreading the mist's influence wherever it stalks.*

**Motives and Tactics:** Spread mist corruption, support other mist creatures, create zones of danger, avoid direct combat

**Difficulty:** 13
**Thresholds:** 6/12
**HP:** 4
**Stress:** 4
**ATK:** +1 **Mist Projector:** Close range — 1d6+1 mag
**Experience:** Heat Sense +2

## Features

### Heat Seekers — Passive
They are the hounds sent out by the mist to seek out flames and extinguish them. Mist Throwers prioritize fire, light sources, and warm-blooded creatures when selecting targets.

### Mist Spray — Action
The Mist Thrower releases a cloud of corrosive mist in a Close range area. Make a separate attack roll against each target in the area. Adversaries with the Heat Seekers feature are immune. On a success, targets take 1d6+1 mag damage.

### Spread the Mist — Action
Mark a Stress to have the Mist Thrower create a cloud of thick otherworldly mist in a Close range area that lasts until the end of the scene. Any creature that starts their turn inside the fog or enters it for the first time on their turn gains a Mist token. PCs may use a Reaction to make an Instinct roll against Difficulty 13 to resist. While this fog cloud is active, mist creatures can enter areas protected by Sacred Flames without penalty.

---

*These were once engineers, miners, or divers who relied on protective equipment to survive harsh environments. Now corrupted by the mist, they use their knowledge of pressure systems and containment to weaponize the very fog that consumed them, spreading its influence to new victims.*
