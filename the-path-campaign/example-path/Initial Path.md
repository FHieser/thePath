# Initial Path

## Route

Village in the Mist

**Outbound:**
Windswept Grasslands → Ancient Crossing → Forest Refuge (Jagged Knife Fight) → Sacred Mountain Pass

**Return:**
Sacred Mountain Pass → Forest Refuge (Watcher NPC) → Ancient Crossing → Windswept Grasslands (Mist Born Fight) → Village in the Mist

---

## Special Encounters

### Forest Refuge (Outbound) - Jagged Knife Fight
- 1x Jagged Knife Lieutenant
- 3x Jagged Knife Bandit
- 1x Jagged Knife Kneebreaker
- Marauders occupy the mysterious dwelling

### Forest Refuge (Return) - Watcher NPC
- The Watcher emerges after marauders cleared
- Offers tea, medicine, health potion
- Silent Rootbound guardian

### Windswept Grasslands (Return) - Mist Born Fight
- 1x Mist Wraith Leader
- 2x Mist Stalker
- 32x Mist Walker (minions - 1 hit each)
