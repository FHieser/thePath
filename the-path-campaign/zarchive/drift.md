# Drift

**Faction:** Factionless (Leader of "the Lost" bandit crew)
**Location:** Trading Grounds (watchtower area)
**Role:** Bandit leader desperately trying to secure permanent sanctuary

---

## Appearance
- Obsessively wrapped in layers of cloth/leather to prevent mist exposure
- Eyes glow faintly with blue-green hue (early Mist Born transformation)
- Too busy keeping the Lost alive to worry about it

## The Twist
- Still sends resources to dying Rootbound guardian and survivors at Burnt Clearing
- Robs for Flame Essence to light Trading Grounds' watchtower brazier
- Trying to create Stable sanctuary for survivors
- Guardian represents having a protector - wants to recreate that

## Theme in Relation to PCs
- **Survival vs. Morality**
- Lost sanctuary to Mist Born, now robs to save survivors
- Mirrors: "What would you do if your village was gone?"
- Forces judgment on whether survival justifies theft

## What They Want
- Flame Essence (light watchtower brazier, make Trading Grounds Stable)
- Information about sanctuaries or creating permanent refuges
- The Lost and Burnt Clearing survivors to have a home

## What They Offer
- Toll-based passage (1 Flame Essence OR supplies = safe crossing)
- Uneasy truce (if PCs trustworthy, might share Trading Grounds or ally)

## Combat Statistics
**Jagged Knife Lieutenant** (Tier 1 Leader)

---

## GM Notes
- Prefers negotiation - fighting wastes resources
- Only fights if PCs refuse all deals or threaten the Lost
- Becomes emotional if PCs mention Burnt Clearing/guardian
- Can become ally if PCs help secure sanctuary
