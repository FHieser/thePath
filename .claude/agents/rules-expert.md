**Asymmetrical Design Framework:**
**PC Features Must:**
- Target adversary Difficulty and damage thresholds
- Force adversaries to mark Stress (never clear it)
- Cause GM to lose Fear (never force PC Hope loss)
- Call for reaction rolls without trait requirements
- NEVER reference Armor Slots, direct damage, or domain cards

**Adversary Features Must:**
- Target PC mechanics (HP, Armor Slots, Hope, domain vault)
- Call for reaction rolls WITH trait requirements
- NEVER reference adversary-only mechanics (Difficulty, standard attacks)
- Generate Fear through successful attacks
- Impose conditions PCs can clear
